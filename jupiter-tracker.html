how this came to be

Vikram opened the Jupiter session with an auction. Everyone got 100 coins to bid on financial security, recognition, pleasure, good fortune, protection, wisdom, children. Some bid hard and ran out of coins. Some held back. Then a "market crash" cut everyone's money in half — and humility went on sale. The whole game was Jupiter: grace, abundance, the regret of missed opportunities, the realisation that what felt like a loss was sometimes a blessing.

Vikram's pivot: "these planets are facets of us, not something out there." So the eight sliders below let you sit with where Jupiter is in you right now — false expectation on one end, divine grace on the other. Read the full session →
