Aspects · the gaze

Place any planet in any chamber. Watch where it looks. Mars reaches further than the 7th. So does Jupiter. So does Saturn. Drishti is the act of looking — and looking transfers energy.

Vikram's intent

"By the act of looking, energy is transferred to whatever is in that region. The aspect can be benefic or malefic — it depends on what is doing the looking."

— Vikram · Aspects · the principle

how to play

Pick a planet · tap a sign · see the gaze

Every planet aspects the 7th chamber from itself — straight across. Three planets — Mars, Jupiter, Saturn — also reach further. The chamber count starts from the planet's own sign as 1; move forward in the zodiac.
