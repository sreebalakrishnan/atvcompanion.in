A simple, honest model for keeping the work going. Free for the first month — try every page, every section, every prompt. After that, a small monthly amount that covers hosting, fonts, and continued work.

One price. No tiers. No feature locks. Free first month, then the same monthly amount for everyone. You can cancel anytime; if you do, your access continues until the end of the month you've paid for.

What's included

Everything in the app. There is no free tier and paid tier — there's just the app, supported.

  • Learning Companion — the dense reference. Twelve signs, nine planets, twelve houses, ten avatars, classical material from BPHS and Uttara Kalamrita, all cross-linked.
  • Interpret · beta — pick your Lagna, place your planets, walk through Vikram's eight-step procedure for analysing a house. Notes save in your browser.
  • Reflect — workbook reflection prompts per avatar, drafted by Marie, Sudha, Mannat & Spandana with Vikram, Megha & Neha.
  • Peer pages — Kaalapurusha, Mercury Spectrum, Astronomy, Sripati Paddhati, Ether, Layers, Interactive Lab. Updated as Vikram's class moves.
  • Future pages — anything new that gets built lands here without a separate charge.
