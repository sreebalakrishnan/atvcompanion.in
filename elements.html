The four elements

An exercise Vikram ran four times across the year — Earth in Session 11, Water in 13, Fire in 17, Air in 18b. Each time the same shape: read a Sri Aurobindo passage, pull "three to five phrases that call you," translate them into image, song, food, brand, animal, news. The cohort filled break-out rooms with what those phrases became. This page brings it back.

Vikram's intent

"Images can be very powerful way of connecting with planets, signs, houses, elements… that itself will be a gateway to your intuition. That is what we are trying to tap into — intuition, not memory."

— Vikram, Session 17 · September 7, 2025

How to use this with the class

Element as filter, not definition

Vikram, Session 18b: "use this as a filter for your day-to-day life — what is your brand, which element does it fall under?" The cohort learned to read the world through these four. The aim isn't to memorise; it's to recognise.

When you sit with your own chart later, the Sun's element, the Moon's element, the Lagna's element — those won't be flashcards anymore. They'll be moods you've already noticed, songs you've already been carrying. That's the whole point.
