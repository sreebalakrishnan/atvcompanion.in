The Planetary Cabinet

Vikram's story of the planets as a royal court, laid out as a South Indian chart. Each sign is a chamber; each planet has a role. Switch modes to see where the planets sit, where they're exalted (uchcha), where they're debilitated (neecha) — or run the story scene by scene.
