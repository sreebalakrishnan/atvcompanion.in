# Reflective Prompts — Astrology 101

**Source:** peer-distilled from Vikram's classes (extracted by another agent, supplied 5 May 2026).
**Voice:** simpler and more accessible than the deck-native prompts. Designed for journaling and reflection, not deck recall.
**Coverage:** Dualities, Modalities, four Elements (with Mahashaktis), nine of the ten Avatars (Matsya through Krishna; Buddha and Kalki not in this batch), and one Astronomy / General prompt.

These complement the deck-native prompts already in `avatars/NN-name.md` (Self-reflection prompts section). Keep both — the peer prompts are friendlier; the deck prompts are closer to Vikram's original wording.

## Dualities & Modalities

### Dualities · Yin / Yang
- How comfortable are you receiving support?
- Does inner alignment or external validation motivate you?
- In which area should you slow down?
- What is your relationship with "productivity"?
- Is your sensitivity a strength or a burden?

### Modalities · Cardinal / Fixed / Mutable
- When do you feel most alive: starting, persevering, or completing?
- Do you struggle with sticking to one thing?
- What does "commitment" mean to you?
- Do you value stability over growth?
- Do you lose interest once the excitement wears off?

## Elements — Fire, Earth, Air, Water

### Fire · Mahakali
- When have you felt brave like a warrior?
- Think of a time you felt deeply about something; what inner obstacles (asuras) did you burn away?
- If you felt full of energy to change everything, what positive change could you start today?
- Have you ever used anger to do something good?
- What is your big dream, and what small step can you take toward it today?

### Earth · Mahasaraswati
- Where in your life do you feel strong and steady like a mountain?
- What small thing can you do to stay grounded and calm when life is busy?
- Is there something you are holding too tightly that you could let go of?
- When do you show care and patience, and how does it feel to take your time to do something well?

### Air · Maheshwari
- When or where do you feel free in your life?
- When was the last time you used your intelligence to help others?
- If you feel scattered, what would bring clarity?
- How can you be more open-minded in difficult situations?
- What does "seeing the big picture" mean to you?

### Water · Mahalakshmi
- Where do you feel soft and gentle, and how can you bring that to other parts of yourself?
- What helped you feel true bliss, and how can you invite it more often?
- What parts of your life are in harmony, and how can you create that for others?
- Is there a worry you are keeping inside that you could share?
- When overwhelmed, what helps you feel calm again?

## The Ten Avatars (Dashavatara)

### 1. Matsya · Ketu
- Can we allow a Higher Force to lead us in difficulty?
- Can we stay detached and perhaps lose something to find something new?
- Is the universe teaching us something when things fall apart?
- How can we welcome a full reset in our lives?
- Can we have faith in the illogical?
- What are we clinging to, and how can we release our grip?

### 2. Kurma · Saturn
- What or who acts as your "stability"?
- Do you have a goal requiring a "slow and steady" approach?
- What are the duties for which you expect no reward?
- What are you carrying that feels like a burden?
- What is your version of a "shell" for rest or protection?
- What experiences have humbled you?

### 3. Varaha · Rahu
- What part of you feels buried in chaos or confusion, and how can you retrieve it?
- Have you ever been treated like an outsider for a path you chose?
- When have you mistaken illusion for truth?
- Do you have desires that make you "sink to the bottom"?
- Are you amplifying anything out of proportion?

### 4. Narasimha · Mars
- Has aggressive energy been constructive or destructive for you?
- Have you stood up for a belief despite opposition?
- How do you react to injustice?
- Do you search for binary answers (yes/no) or can you live with "shades of grey"?

### 5. Vamana · Jupiter
- Recall a moment when kindness ruled over your actions; did it change your view of power?
- Have you ever protected someone who once opposed you?
- Have your desires ever outgrown what you could hold?

### 6. Parashuram · Venus
- How does devotion and loyalty enhance your connections with others?
- How often do you seek happiness from external sources?
- What mindsets cultivate inner fulfilment?
- Have you balanced opposites (like penance and action) in your life?

### 7. Rama · Sun
- Have you ever given up comfort to do what is right?
- What values do you refuse to compromise?
- What gives you the strength to face challenges?

### 8. Krishna · Moon
- What is your earliest memory of being nurtured?
- When triggered, what could you have done to stay balanced?
- How can your capacity to guide others be strengthened?
- How easy is it for you to empathise with others?
- What does "unconditional love" mean to you?
