# Deck 61: Kinds of Houses (continued)

**Source file:** `61. Kinds of Houses (continued).pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses

## Topic
A continuation of the Houses overview, mapping the 12 Houses to life areas, then layering Trikonas (trines), the four Purusharthas (aims of life), and Sanskrit House names onto the chart. Concludes with the Kaalapurusha concept (Aries-as-Ascendant default chart).

## Key concepts
- 12-House life-area map: 1H Self, 2H Resources/Family/Support, 3H Hobbies/Siblings/Effort, 4H Home/Mother/Emotions/Fixed assets, 5H Creations/Children/Romance, 6H Health/Competitors/Diseases/Uncle, 7H Commitments/Spouse/Business, 8H Challenges/Obstacles/Longevity, 9H Learning/Teacher/Higher education/Long travel, 10H Awards/Recognition/Friends/Status, 11H Government/Father/Boss/Work (note: deck swaps the typical 10H/11H labels), 12H Expenses/Subconscious/Morning practice
- Trikonas (1, 5, 9): most supportive houses; relate to purpose, wisdom, values, past karma; planets here express higher qualities; show meaning, direction, inner compass
- Purusharthas (4 aims of life) mapped to House groups:
  - Dharma (1, 5, 9) — righteousness, moral duties (Chandogya Upanishad)
  - Artha (2, 6, 10) — material wealth, prosperity (Brihadaranyaka Upanishad)
  - Kama (3, 7, 11) — desires, emotions, love, art (Kathopanishad)
  - Moksha (4, 8, 12) — liberation, transcendence (Mandukya and Taittiriya Upanishad)
- Kaalapurusha: the cosmic person; chart taken with Aries as Ascendant as the default reference

- Putra — Children (5H)
- Rog — Disease (6H)

- Aayu / Mrityu — Longevity / Death (8H)
- Bhagya — Good Fortune (9H)
- Karma — Action (10H)
- Labha — Gains (11H)
- Vyaya — Expenses (12H)
- Trikona — trine
- Purushartha — aim of human life
