# Deck 21: Mahasaraswathi

**Source file:** `21. Mahasaraswathi.txt`
**Type:** Goddess / Mythology (Tridevi / Earth element)

## Topic
Introduces Mahasaraswati as one of the four great Aspects of the Mother (per Sri Aurobindo), associated with the Earth element and the zodiac signs grouped as Earth signs.

## Key concepts
- Element grouping of zodiac: Earth (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn), Water (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces), Fire (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius), Air (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius).
- The four Powers/Personalities of the Mother: Maheshwari, Mahakali, Mahalakshmi, Mahasaraswati (per Sri Aurobindo, "The Mother").
- Mahasaraswati is the Power of skill, science, craft, technique — the tireless builder, organiser, administrator, technician, artisan, and classifier of the worlds.
- Her action is laborious, minute, persistent, integral, flawless; she is patient with human imperfections; supports those who are single-willed and sincere.
- Group exercises: each break-out room finds an artwork / movie clip / famous person / song that embodies Earth qualities.
- Self-reflection prompts on Earth qualities: feeling grounded like a mountain; staying calm amid busyness; releasing what is held too tightly; care and patience in work; choosing a quality to grow this month.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Mahasaraswati — Mother as skill, perfection, organised action
- Maheshwari, Mahakali, Mahalakshmi (named alongside, fully covered in deck 25)

## Names / references mentioned
- Sri Aurobindo, "The Mother" (pp. 20, 28)
- Vikram Devatha (allthingsvedic.in)

## Notes
Pattern (shared with deck 25): introduce one of four Mother aspects, link to its element, give Aurobindo's prose description, run group exercises, end with self-reflection prompts. Mahasaraswati is paired with Earth in this deck.
