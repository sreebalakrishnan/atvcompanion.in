**Source file:** `1. Plan for the year.txt`

## Topic
Course-opening orientation deck for the Astrology 101 program. Covers the year-long structure (online classes + retreats + mentor sessions + self work), retreat dates, tools, etiquette, and a self-paced online option.

## Key concepts
- Program has four learning streams: Astronomy, Indian Philosophy, Mythology, Self work, all feeding into Astrology
- Two delivery modes: Online classes and Retreats
- Online classes: Sundays 2:30 - 4:30 pm IST, 2 hours/week; first class 11 May 2025
- Self work: 1-2 hours/week, scheduled with mentors
- Mentor sessions: 1-hour, small groups of 6-7 learners, online or offline, not recorded
- Retreats: ~15 days spread over the year, INR 10-12k per retreat for accommodation and food, 2-3 facilitators, simple guesthouses, vegetarian, Thursday check-in to Sunday check-out
- Five element-based retreats plus an end-of-year retreat (dates listed below)
- Astrology 101 Self Paced is a parallel LMS-based version with quizzes, app, community forum, video + transcripts, software discounts; complimentary for this group
- Etiquette: be 10 min early, do not share decks/recordings, do not read others' charts, webcam on, share publicly only if relevant else DM
