# Deck 71: Starting Interpretations

**Source file:** `71. Starting interpretations.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Course recap and framing

## Topic
A meta / recap deck that summarizes everything covered so far in the course (Astronomy, Mythology, Astrology) and frames how chart interpretation works — using a language-learning analogy and a "puzzle" / "science vs doctor" metaphor. Establishes important rules for the practitioner.

## Key concepts
- Three-pillar curriculum recap:
  - Astronomy: Equinoxes/Solstices, Moon phases, Planet-in-Sign, Coordinate systems, Stellarium, Precession, Retrograde, Nodes/Eclipses
  - Mythology: Vyasa, the 10 Avatars (Matsya, Kurma, Varaha, Narasimha, Vamana, Parashuram, Rama, Krishna, Buddha), Stories of the Temples
  - Astrology: Dualities/Modalities/Elements, Relationships and Aspects, Houses, Retreats (Earth/Water/Fire/Air/Ether), Reading the chart, Navagrahas, Combustion, Retrograde, Yogas, Eclipses
- Heart and Mind framing
- Language-learning analogy: Words → Sentences → Paragraphs → Chapters → Book → Comparing books (mirroring the build-up of interpretive complexity)
- "To describe a puzzle" framing
- Science vs Doctor / Astrology vs Astrologer distinction (the practitioner is more than the discipline)
- Question: Do planets filter the stars, or is it synchronicity?
- Channelling guidance: keep the pipe clean, create a sacred space, connect to the Divine
- Important rule: NO predictions, and NO reading the charts of others
- Schedule references: 9th, 10th, 11th, 12th, 13th April

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Navagrahas — nine planets (recap reference)
- Yogas — planetary combinations (recap reference)

Deck is a syllabus/philosophy bridge before deeper interpretation work. The "no predictions, no reading others' charts" rule is explicit and important. Deck is mostly text fragments and bullet points — sparse but high signal.
