# Deck 14: Dualities

**Source file:** `14. Dualities.txt`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals (sign classification)

## Topic
Introduces the duality classification of the 12 zodiac signs (masculine/feminine, or Yin/Yang). Process-of-division framing: One-ness → 12 letters → grouped into pairs. Closes with self-reflection prompts and example remedies for Yin vs Yang.

## Key concepts
- "Process of Division": One-ness fragmented into 12 (letters A-L), reorganized into the 12 signs
- Signs split into two dualities (alternating around the wheel):
  - Yang / Masculine: Aries, Gemini, Leo, Libra, Sagittarius, Aquarius (the 1st, 3rd, 5th, 7th, 9th, 11th)
  - Yin / Feminine: Taurus, Cancer, Virgo, Scorpio, Capricorn, Pisces (the 2nd, 4th, 6th, 8th, 10th, 12th)
  - (Note: deck shows the 12 signs around the wheel without explicit Yin/Yang labels in the extracted text — the duality is understood from context and made explicit in Deck 15)
- 12 self-reflection prompts (numbered) about receiving help, motivation, slowing down, holding back, rest, emotional expression, productivity, sensitivity, competition, vulnerability, pushing too hard, withdrawing
- Example remedies grouped under "To support Yin" / "To support Yang" (extracted text shows headers only — details fleshed out in Deck 15)

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Duality — sign polarity (yin/yang, masculine/feminine)
