## Key concepts (in addition to Deck 4)
- 12-month sunrise photo (sunrise position drifts north-south through the year)
- Ecliptic — apparent path of the Sun
- Time per degree calculation:
  - 1 rotation = 24 hours = 360 degrees
  - 15 degrees of arc per hour (360/24)
  - 1 degree of arc per 4 minutes (60/15)
  - Worked example: Auroville zenith vs. Bangalore zenith time gap
- IST set at 82°30'E
- Time formats: Local Mean Time (LMT), Zonal Standard Time, Daylight Saving Time, War Time (1942-1945), Mecca Time
- Practice questions:
  - LMT at 6:15pm at 10°S 75°W → time at 30°N 75°W (same longitude, no change)
  - If Earth stopped revolving at next solstice — energy consumption, crime rate effects (open-ended)
