# Deck 26: Dreams

**Source file:** `26. Dreams.txt`
**Type:** Practice / Self-work (Dreamwork)

## Topic
A practice-oriented deck inviting students to begin dream journaling as preparation for an upcoming Water retreat. Frames dreams as a portal into the intuitive water-element realm.

## Key concepts
- Dreamwork as a portal into intuition — the realm Water represents.
- Dreams may carry messages revealing what we are not seeing, or hints from the soul guiding us forward.
- Practice: keep a dream journal (notebook, voice recorder, or sketchbook) by the bed in the lead-up to the Water retreat.
- The most important first step is the intention to remember dreams before going to sleep — even chronic non-rememberers are often surprised.
- On waking (morning or middle of the night), record immediately before the mind dismisses fragments as trivial.
- Honour even short fragments — when honoured, dreams reveal more of themselves over time.
