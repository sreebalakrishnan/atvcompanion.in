# Deck 34: Signature

**Source file:** `34. Signature.txt`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals

## Topic
Introduces the concept of a chart "Signature" - the dominant Duality + Modality + Element combination across the Navagrahas (nine planets), which identifies a single representative Sign for the chart.

## Key concepts
- Method: tally Yang/Yin, Cardinal/Fixed/Mutable, and Element (Fire/Earth/Air/Water) across all nine grahas based on the Sign each planet sits in.
- Worked example tallies (sample chart):
  - Sun: Yang, Fixed, Fire
  - Moon: Yang, Mutable, Air
  - Mars: Yang, Mutable, Fire
  - Mercury: Yin, Mutable, Earth
  - Jupiter: Yang, Mutable, Air
  - Venus: Yin, Cardinal, Water
  - Saturn: Yin, Fixed, Earth
  - Rahu: Yang, Mutable, Air
  - Ketu: Yang, Mutable, Fire
- Aggregates: Yang 6, Yin 3; Cardinal 1, Fixed 2, Mutable 6; Fire 3, Earth 2, Air 3, Water 1.
- Since Mutable dominates and Fire/Air tie at 3, the Signature is **Mutable Fire (Sagittarius)** or **Mutable Air (Gemini)**.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Navagraha - the nine planets (Sun, Moon, Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Rahu, Ketu)
- Signature - dominant Duality/Modality/Element profile yielding a representative Sign
