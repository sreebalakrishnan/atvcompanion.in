# Deck 28: Sun & Moon

**Source file:** `28. Sun & Moon.txt`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals (Luminaries, Zodiac, Calendar)

## Topic
Introduces the Sun and Moon as the two luminaries: Sun moving through the zodiacal signs across the year, Moon's waxing/waning cycle, and how key Indian festivals correspond to the Sun's sign ingresses.

## Key concepts
- The Zodiacal Signs introduced.
- South Indian Chart format introduced (visual reference to Aries, Taurus, Gemini placement).
- Moon phases: waxing vs waning.
- Sun's motion through the signs of the zodiac across the year, with approximate ingress dates (sankrantis):
  - 14 Jan, 13 Feb, 15 Mar, 14 Apr, 15 May, 15 Jun, 16 Jul, 17 Aug, 17 Sep, 17 Oct, 16 Nov, 16 Dec.
- Festivals tied to Sun positions:
  - Makar Sankranti (14 Jan, Sun into Capricorn)
  - Ugadi (around 15 Mar)
  - Tamil New Year (14 Apr, Sun into Aries)
  - Guru Purnima (mid-year full moon reference)
- Practice slide pairing Sun-sign and Moon-state (e.g., Sun in Leo / Full Moon in Aquarius; Sun in Virgo / New Moon in Virgo; Sun in Capricorn / Full Moon in Cancer; Sun in Gemini / New Moon in Gemini; Sun in Aries / Waxing Moon in Cancer; Sun in Scorpio / Waning Moon in Gemini; Sun in Leo / Waning Moon in Pisces; Sun in Taurus / Waxing Moon in Libra) — Q (quarter) and R (full/round?) markings on each.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Sankranti — Sun's ingress into a new zodiac sign
- Makar Sankranti, Ugadi, Guru Purnima — festivals tied to solar/lunar positions
- South Indian Chart — fixed-house chart format

## Names / references mentioned

- Vikram Devatha (allthingsvedic.in)
