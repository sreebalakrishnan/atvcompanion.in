# Deck 50: Aspects

**Source file:** `50. Aspects.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals

## Topic
Introduces Drishti - astrological aspects. Covers Sign aspects (Rasi Drishti) and Planetary aspects (Graha Drishti) including special aspects for Mars, Jupiter, and Saturn, plus the orb of influence.

## Key concepts
- An aspect = "looking at" a region of space; the act of looking transfers energy to that region. Aspects can be benefic or malefic.
- Rasi Drishti / Sign Aspects (BPHS 8:1-5):
  - Cardinal signs (and planets in them) aspect all Fixed signs except the one adjacent.
  - Fixed signs aspect all Cardinal signs except the one adjacent.
  - Mutable signs aspect all other Mutable signs.
- Graha Drishti / Planetary Aspects (BPHS 26:2-5):
  - All planets aspect the 7th (count from the aspecting planet's sign as 1, clockwise).
  - Rahu and Ketu also aspect the 7th, but never each other.
  - Special aspects:
    - Mars aspects 4th and 8th.
    - Jupiter aspects 5th and 9th.
    - Saturn aspects 3rd and 10th.
- Orb of influence (intensity falls off with distance from exact degree):
  - Exact degree = full aspect.
  - +/- 0 to 2.5 deg = moderate, 3/4 aspect.
  - +/- 2.5 to 5 deg = medium, 1/2 aspect.
  - +/- 5 to 7.5 deg = weakest, 1/4 aspect.
  - Standard orb is approximately +/- 7.5 deg, but varies by software.
- Caveat: aspects shown by software may not match BPHS exactly - "It is not clear how this software is calculating aspects."
- Try-it: identify which planets aspect each planet in your chart (planet aspects only); note any mutual aspects.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Drishti / Dhrishti — aspect, "to look".
- Rasi Drishti — sign aspect.
- Graha Drishti — planetary aspect.

## Names / references mentioned
- BPHS 8:1-5 (sign aspects); BPHS 26:2-5 (planetary aspects, orb).
