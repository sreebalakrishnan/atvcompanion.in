# Deck 72: Sun

**Source file:** `72. Sun.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Planet (Surya)

## Topic
Comprehensive deck on the Sun: its astronomy, classical significations, planetary aspects and yogas, and an interpretive method ("Parts of Speech") that maps grammatical roles onto astrological factors to generate sentences about a chart placement. Concludes with one-liner interpretations of Sun in each Sign.

## Key concepts
- Sun astronomy: largest, mightiest body; source of light, energy, life; holds everything in place; takes 365 days for 360 deg; rises slightly to north/south each day
- Karaka chart for the Sun across the 12 Houses (visual)
- Bhagavad Gita 2.12-13, 2.22 quotes on the eternal soul; Chandogya Upanishad 5.10.7 on rebirth by deeds
- Ramayana symbolic decoding (associated with the Sun = Soul):
  - Ra-Ma = the light within / Paramatma
  - Dasharatha = 10 chariots = sense organs and organs of action
  - Kausalya = skillfully; Ayodhya = no conflict, find inner light
  - Sita = Mind / Prakriti / Jivatma
  - Ravana (10 heads) = ego — Prakriti kidnapped by Ego
  - Hanuman = son of Vayu = breath / Life-Force / Prana
  - Lakshmana = Ananta Sesha = awareness
- Reflection prompts: Dharma (purpose), Just/noble ruler (leadership qualities), Courage and Valor (what gives strength)
- Classical significations:
  - Parashara: Sun = Soul; honey-coloured eyes, square body, clean habits, bilious, intelligent, limited hair
  - Phala Deepika: self, father, influence, health, vitality, fortune; copper, gold, anything auspicious; worship of Shiva, performer of sacrifice, king, minister, tiger, deer, ruddy goose
  - Uttara Kalamrita: extensive list — soul, self-realization, power, courage, fortress, kingdom, fire, heat, splendor, father, king, Eastern direction, copper, blood, satvika, etc.
- Planetary Aspects: Sun aspects only the 7th (BPHS 26:2-5)
- Yogas and combinations involving the Sun:
  - Vesi (planets in 2H from Sun): refined, resourceful, builds wealth (BPHS 39)
  - Vasi (planets in 12H from Sun): discipline, restraint, thoughtful action
  - Ubhayachari (planets on both sides): balanced, capable, strong character
  - Neecha Bhanga Raja Yoga: Sun debilitated but in Kendra from Moon → success after struggle, strong leadership (BPHS 40)
  - Budha Aditya Yoga: Sun + Mercury together → intelligence, skilful speech, sharp mind (BPHS Ch 13-15)
- "Parts of Speech" interpretation framework:
  - Noun = Planet (what is acting)
  - Verb = Duality / Yang-Yin (direction of expression)
  - Adverb = Modality / Cardinal-Fixed-Mutable (how the action happens)
  - Adjective = Element / Fire-Earth-Air-Water (quality of the action)
  - Area of life = House (where it shows up)
  - Worked example: Sun in Aries 2H → "The self shines by leading the family with warmth"
- Four interpretive intents (same fact, four framings):
  - Predict: "You will be confident in public speaking"
  - Initiate conversation: "Have you ever spoken to an audience?"
  - Invite course of action: "Can you try presenting what you know?"
  - Remedy the planet: "Consider presenting what you know"
- Sun in each Sign (one-liners): Aries — confident in leading transformations; Taurus — grounded in commitments; Gemini — proactively adapt communication; Cancer — peacefully assert emotions; Leo — enthusiastically focus on passion; Virgo — gently and patiently collaborate; Libra — confident and assertive in communicating; Scorpio — grounded and resilient in emotions; Sagittarius — ambitious and adaptable in creativity; Capricorn — patiently set practical and grounded goals; Aquarius — proactively persevere towards freedom; Pisces — intuitively respond to truth
