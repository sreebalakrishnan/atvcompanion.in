# Deck 9: Distance, Time & Speed

**Source file:** `9. Distance, Time & Speed.txt`
**Type:** Astronomy fundamentals / Practice

## Topic
Practice deck applying degree-time arithmetic. Bridges everyday distance/time/speed (km, hours) with celestial distance/time/speed (degrees, minutes of arc).

## Key concepts
- Conversion units recap: 1 km = 1000 m; 1 m = 100 cm; 1 hour = 60 min; 1 min = 60 sec
- Concrete example: Bangalore to Mumbai
  - via NH48: 989 km, 17h 19m → 58 km/hr
  - via NH50: 1003 km, 18h 18m → 55 km/hr
- Arc analogue: 1° = 60'; 1' = 60''; 1 hr = 60 min = 3600 sec
- Earth rotation: 360° in 24 hr → 15°/hr → 1° per 4 min
- Bangalore (77.6°E) - Mumbai (72.7°E) → arc = 4.9° → 4.9 × 4 min = 19.6 min time difference (LMT)
- 7 worked LMT problems (longitude shifts, east/west adjustment, latitude irrelevant)
- Open-ended pair questions: Earth stopping at solstice (energy, crime), Earth revolving in opposite direction (sunrise direction), humans controlling tilt (geopolitics), shadow length through the year

## Worked LMT examples
- 0°W noon → 30°E = 2:00 PM
- 45°W 3 PM → 15°W = 5:00 PM
- 90°E 6 AM → 60°E = 4:00 AM
- 75°E 9 AM → 45°W = 1:00 AM
- 120°E 10 PM → 60°W = 10:00 AM
- Same longitude regardless of latitude → same LMT (4:00 PM at 20°N 60°E = 4:00 PM at 40°S 60°E)
- 6:15 PM at 10°S 75°W → 30°N 75°W = 6:15 PM (same longitude)
