# Deck 13: Temples of India

**Source file:** `13. Temples of India.txt`
**Type:** Indian Philosophy / Practical (temple etiquette + Nakshatra/Gotra)

## Topic
Survey of Indian temple architectural styles through history, then practical guidance on temple visits — what to share with the priest (name, Nakshatra, Gotra), and how to find your Nakshatra and Gotra.

## Key concepts

**Architectural timeline:**
- Pre-5000 BC: Yajnas and nature worship (no temples)
- 200 BC - 1000 AD: Rock-cut shrines — Ajanta, Ellora, Elephanta, Badami caves
- 500-1000 AD: Nagara style — Khajuraho, Orissa, Rajasthan, MP
- 600-1000 AD: Dravida style — Pallavas, Cholas
- 700-1400 AD: Vesara style — Chalukyas, Hoysalas (Badami, Belur, Halebidu)
- 600-1300 AD: Kalinga style — Jagannath (Puri), Lingaraja, Konark Sun Temple
- 800 AD onwards: Himalayan style — Badrinath, Kedarnath
- 1200-1800 AD: Temple cities — Vijayanagara & Nayaka (Hampi)
- 1900 AD onwards: Modern temples — Ramakrishna Temple Chennai, Birla, ISKCON, etc.

**Dravida (South Indian) temples:**
- Pandya, Pallava, Chola dynasties
- Designed using Agama and Vasthu Shastra
- Layout: outer to inner = gross to subtle
- The body is mapped to the temple

**Temple visit etiquette:**
- Remove footwear, set intentions
- Offer respect to Nandi, speak intention; 3 clockwise rounds of temple; no photos beyond
- For puja, share name + Nakshatra + Gotra with priest
- Connect with the Divine

**Nakshatra (in this context):**
- Translates to "star"
- Generally refers to the Nakshatra where the Moon is placed at birth
- Use any Vedic Astrology software (e.g., deva.guru) to find it

**Gotra:**
- Family lineage / which Sage (Rishi) family originates from
- 14 main gotras listed: Atri, Bhrigu, Kashyapa, Vasishta, Gautama, Bharadwaja, Vishwamitra, Agastya, Jamadagni, Shandilya, Kaushika, Pulastya, Angirasa, Marichi
- Find via: ask family, traditions, family priest, genealogy registers at Haridwar
- If unknown: use Kashyapa or Vashishta, OR derive "personal" gotra from Moon's Nakshatra
- Personal Gotra mapping (7 Saptarishis):
  1. Marichi → Ashwini, Pushya, Swati
  2. Vasishtha → Bharani, Aslesha, Visakha, Shravana
  3. Angiras → Krittika, Magha, Anuradha, Dhanistha
  4. Atri → Rohini, Purva-Phalguni, Jyeshta, Satabisha
  5. Pulastya → Mrigasira, Uttara-Phalguni, Mula, Purva-Bhadrapada
  6. Pulahu → Ardra, Hasta, Purva-Ashadha, Uttara-Bhadrapada
  7. Kratu → Punarvasu, Chitra, Uttara-Ashadha, Revati
