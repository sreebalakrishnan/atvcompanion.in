# Deck 53: End of Year + Projects

**Source file:** `53. End of Year + Projects.pdf`
**Type:** Logistics / Curriculum recap

## Topic
End-of-year retreat announcement, a recap of everything covered in year one (Astronomy, Mythology, Astrology), and a menu of possible learner projects.

## Key concepts
- End-of-Year retreat: 2-5 July 2026, in Auroville. Sessions led by learners. Take-home: something tangible from the year.
- Year-1 curriculum recap:
  - Astronomy: Equinoxes and Solstices; Phases of the Moon; "Planet in Sign"; Coordinate systems in the Sky; Using Stellarium; Precession of the Equinoxes; Retrograde motion; Nodes of the Moon and Eclipses; Houses.
  - Mythology: Vyasa; Avatars (Matsya, Kurma, Varaha, Narasimha, Vamana, Parashuram, Rama, Krishna, Buddha); Stories of the Temples.
  - Astrology: Dualities, Modalities, Elements; Relationships; Aspects; Retreats on the Elements; Navagrahas; Combustion; Retrograde; Yogas; Eclipses; Houses; Reading your chart.
- Project categories:
  - Study-based: workbook/booklet of concepts/diagrams/Sanskrit terms; one-topic deep-dive video for a beginner or 10-year-old; mind-map poster; quiz for new students; summary notes.
  - Application: blog/podcast on personal application; interviews with fellow students; physical sky-models (eclipses, retrogrades, seasons, lunar phases).
  - Creative: planet/sign/house game (board or card); astrology art; short films (one concept per episode); skit/play; revision/reflection/integration formats.
- Next steps for learners: pick a topic, pick a style, choose solo/buddy/group, share a one-page brief (who/what/why/how/when), schedule check-ins.
