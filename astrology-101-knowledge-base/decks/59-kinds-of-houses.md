# Deck 59: Kinds of Houses

**Source file:** `59. Kinds of Houses.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses

## Topic
Classifies the 12 houses into traditional groupings (Karakas, Kendras, Panaparas, Apoklimas, Upachayas, Dushtanas, Marakas, Trikonas, and the four Purusharthas: Dharma, Artha, Kama, Moksha).

## Key concepts
- Sign chart vs. House chart (recap from Deck 57).
- Karakas (Significators) - one keyword per house used throughout the deck:
  - 1H Ascendant, 2H Wealth, 3H Siblings, 4H Home, 5H Creativity, 6H Diseases, 7H Descendant, 8H Obstacles, 9H Ethics, 10H Career, 11H Status, 12H Expenses.
- Kendras (Angular) - 1, 4, 7, 10:
  - Pillars of the chart. Where life demands action and responsibility (self, home, relationships, work). Planets here express clearly and strongly. About stability, presence, impact.
- Panaparas (Succedent) - 2, 5, 8, 11:
  - Support and sustain what kendras begin. Resources, learning, gains. Build on actions, gather strength, process change. About continuity and nourishment.
- Apoklimas (Cadent) - 3, 6, 9, 12:
  - Effort, service, belief, letting go. Planets here express in the background rather than directly.
- Upachayas (Constant Effort) - 3, 6, 10, 11:
  - Improve with time, effort, experience. Planets here may start weak but strengthen over time. Where struggle leads to progress.
- Dushtanas (Inimical) - 6, 8, 12:
  - Houses of challenge, difficulty, loss. Friction, uncertainty, endings, illness, fear, isolation, letting go. Test resilience and maturity.
- Marakas (Killers) - 2, 7:
  - Linked to endings, especially when reading Dashas. 2H = sustenance/food/family support; 7H = interaction/outward engagement. When activated, can strain life force. Do not mean physical death - mark periods of closure, separation, or weakening.
- Purushartha groupings:
  - Dharma (Righteousness) — Trikonas: 1, 5, 9.
  - Artha (Materialistic Wealth) — 2, 6, 10.
  - Kama (Desires and pleasure) — 3, 7, 11.
  - Moksha (Liberation) — 4, 8, 12.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Karaka — significator.
- Kendra — angular house.
- Panapara — succedent house.
- Apoklima — cadent house.
- Upachaya — house of growth through effort.
- Dushtana — inimical/difficult house.
- Maraka — "killer" house (in Dasha context).
- Trikona — trine (1, 5, 9).
- Dharma, Artha, Kama, Moksha — the four Purusharthas.
- Dasha — planetary period (mentioned in Maraka context).
