# Deck 66: House Systems (ver 3)

**Source file:** `66. House Systems ver 3.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses (technical)

## Topic
Third iteration of the House Systems deck. Adds the full Sripati Paddhathi explanation (built progressively across multiple slides), with the M.C. / Zenith / Ecliptic geometry and worked numerical example.

## Key concepts
- Full Sign and Equal House content carried over from deck 65
- Sripati Paddhathi System (new in this version):
  - Builds the picture in stages: ecliptic crosses the longitude at M.C. (Medium Coeli), the zenith point directly overhead
  - Ascendant is on the eastern horizon; Descendant opposite
  - Mid points of Houses 1, 10, 11, 12 are then identified along the ecliptic
- Sripati worked example (22 deg Aquarius rising, 10th cusp at 25 deg Scorpio = 235 deg):
  - Cusp of 1st House = 322 deg
  - Cusp of 10th House = 235 deg
  - Extent of division = (322 - 235) / 3 = 29 deg
  - 12th cusp = 322 - 29 = 293 deg (23 Cap)
  - 11th cusp = 293 - 29 = 264 deg (24 Sag)
  - To get House start/end points, take midpoints between cusps: end of 12H / start of 1H = (322+293)/2 = 307.5 deg; end of 11H / start of 12H = (293+264)/2 = 278.5 deg

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Sripati Paddhathi — quadrant-based House system using cusps as midpoints
- M.C. (Medium Coeli) — highest point of ecliptic, 10th cusp
- Cusp — midpoint of a House (in Sripati)
