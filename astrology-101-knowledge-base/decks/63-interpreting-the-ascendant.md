# Deck 63: Interpreting the Ascendant

**Source file:** `63. Interpreting the Ascendant.pdf`
**Type:** Lab / Case Study (homework)

## Topic
A homework exercise guiding students to interpret their own Ascendant (Lagna) using a structured 3-step method: factual statement → flashcard keywords → interpretive sentences. A worked example for a Cancer Ascendant is provided.

## Key concepts
- Step 1: Write factual statements about the Ascendant — what Sign rises, who its lord is, where the lord is placed, what House that placement falls in
- Step 2: Note keywords from flashcards for each factual element (Sign duality, modality, element; Lord avatar; House meaning)
- Step 3: Connect factual elements into interpretive sentences
- Do NOT consider aspects at this stage
- Submission: Word doc (not PDF) in the Case Studies folder

## Worked example (Cancer Ascendant)
- Ascendant Cancer, lord Moon in Pisces (9th House)
- Cancer = yin, cardinal, water → introvert, leader, emotions
- Moon = Krishna avatar
- Pisces = yin, mutable, water → introvert, adaptable, emotional
- 9th House = higher education
- Sample sentences: "I am emotional", "I am a leader", "My identity is linked to my higher education", "I nurture and protect my feelings"
