# Deck 62: House Systems

**Source file:** `62. House Systems.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses (technical)

## Topic
Introduces the three House systems used in this course: Full Sign, Equal House, and Sripati Paddhathi. This is version 1 of the House Systems deck (later iterated as decks 65, 66, 69). Covers the Full Sign and Equal House mechanics with worked examples; Sripati appears only as a list item here.

## Key concepts
- Three House systems listed: Full Sign, Equal House, Sripati Paddhathi
- Full Sign House System:
  - Rising Sign = entire 1st House (Ascendant / Lagna)
  - Whole sign = one House
  - Any planet in that Sign is in that House
  - Prescribed by Parasara, Jaimini, Mantreshwara
  - Worked example: 22 deg Aquarius rising → 1st House spans 300-330 deg; 2nd House 330-360 deg
  - Moon in Libra = 9th Sign from Ascendant Aquarius = 9th Bhava
- Equal House System:
  - Rising degree = mid point of 1st House
  - 1st House extends 15 deg either side of Ascendant
  - 2nd House begins 15 deg after Ascendant degree, extends 30 deg
- Distinction: Rasi chart / Sign chart vs. Bhava chart / House chart

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Lagna — Ascendant
- Bhava — House
- Rasi — Sign
- Sripati Paddhathi — a House system (mentioned only)

## Names / references mentioned
- Parasara, Jaimini, Mantreshwara (classical authors prescribing Full Sign)

## Notes
This is the earliest version of the House Systems deck. Sripati Paddhathi is listed but not explained here — it is developed in versions 2, 3, and 4 (decks 65, 66, 69).
