# Deck 47: Apparent Motions

**Source file:** `47. Apparent Motions.pdf`
**Type:** Astronomy / Astrology fundamentals

## Topic
Catalogues the eight types of apparent planetary motion as classified in BPHS, including retrograde and stationary states, plus an exercise on simulating retrograde motion.

## Key concepts
- Eight apparent motions of planets (BPHS 29.21-23):
  - Sama — moving at usual speed
  - Manda — slowing down
  - Mandotar — slowest motion
  - Vikala — stationary
  - Vakra — appears to move back (Retrograde)
  - Char — slightly faster than usual
  - Atichar — enters the next sign in direct motion
  - Anuvakra — enters the previous sign while retrograde
- Try-it exercise: illustrate Mercury or Venus retrograde, identify currently retrograde planets in Stellarium, find which planet retrogrades next.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Sama, Manda, Mandotar, Vikala, Vakra, Char, Atichar, Anuvakra — eight motion states.
