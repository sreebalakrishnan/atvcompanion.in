# Deck 65: House Systems (ver 2)

**Source file:** `65. House systems ver 2.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses (technical)

## Topic
Second iteration of the House Systems deck. Largely the same content as deck 62 but adds visual diagrams showing how multiple consecutive 30-degree segments stack across the ecliptic. Adds an Equal House worked-example table giving start/end degrees of all 12 Houses for a 22 deg Aquarius rising chart.

## Key concepts
- Same Full Sign and Equal House foundations as deck 62
- New: visual ribbon showing successive 30-degree blocks (Start/Mid/End markers) across the ecliptic
- New table: for 22 deg Aquarius rising, all 12 Equal Houses listed (e.g., 1H = 7 Aq to 7 Pis, 2H = 7 Pis to 7 Ar, ..., 12H = 7 Cap to 7 Aq)
- Worked example: Moon in Libra at 10 deg 48' 02" → 7th Sign in Rasi chart (counted from Aries) but 8th House in Bhava chart (counted from Ascendant)

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Same as deck 62: Lagna, Bhava, Rasi, Sripati Paddhathi (still only listed)

## Names / references mentioned
- Parasara, Jaimini, Mantreshwara

## Notes
Successive iteration of deck 62. Sripati Paddhathi still appears only as a header item — not yet developed. Adds the explicit table-of-houses for Equal House and the Rasi-vs-Bhava counting example.
