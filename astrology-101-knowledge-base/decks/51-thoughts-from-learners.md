# Deck 51: Thoughts from Learners

**Source file:** `51. Thoughts from Learners.pdf`
**Type:** Logistics / Learner Feedback

## Topic
A consolidation of feedback from learners on class structure, support systems, and learning aids - used to shape the second half of the year's curriculum.

## Key concepts
- Class structure suggestions:
  - One topic per class.
  - Stories and examples in the second half.
  - Keep questions for the end.
  - Use both South Indian and North Indian chart styles.
  - Importance of revisions.
  - More real-world examples.
- Support structures:
  - Buddy or small-group pairing.
  - Monthly integration or doubt-clearing circle.
  - Oracle cards.
  - Possibility of emailing questions.
- Learning support requested:
  - How to reflect.
  - How to self-study.
  - Orientation to software and technology.
  - Cultural context and time-zone challenges.
- Permission/normalization slides ("It's ok if..."):
  - Life gets in the way and you fall behind.
  - You can't attend all classes.
  - You can't attend retreats.
  - You prefer to listen rather than share.
- Proposed additions: peer learning sessions, a workbook.
