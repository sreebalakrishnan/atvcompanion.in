# Deck 44: Cosmology of Time

**Source file:** `44. Cosmology of Time.txt`
**Type:** Mythology / Cosmology

## Topic
Maps the first six Vishnu avatars to the Navagrahas, gives the Bhagavad Gita verse on divine descent (yada yada hi dharmasya), and lays out the full Vedic time hierarchy from the blink of an eye (Nimisha) up to the lifespan of Brahma, including the Yuga structure and Kali Yuga timeline.

## Key concepts
- **Avatar-to-graha mapping:**
  - Matsya = Ketu (protection, foresight, transformation, preservation of knowledge, divine intervention)
  - Kurma = Saturn (emotional churning, stability, patience, perseverance; Mt Mandara)
  - Varaha = Rahu (going to the depth, protector of the weak, tilling the land)
  - Narasimha = Mars (aggressive energy, witnessing injustice, standing up for someone/something)
  - Vamana = Jupiter (expansiveness, wisdom, law, justice, guardian)
  - Parashurama = Venus (devotion, inner fulfillment, perfection of knowledge)
- **Bhagavad Gita 4.7-4.8 (cited as 2.12-2.13 in deck) on descent of avatars:** "yada yada hi dharmasya glanir bhavati bharata, abhyutthanam adharmasya tadatmanam srijamyaham / paritranaya sadhunam vinashaya cha dushkritam, dharma-samsthapanarthaya sambhavami yuge yuge" - whenever dharma falls and adharma rises, I manifest age after age to protect the righteous, destroy the wicked, and re-establish dharma.
- **Vedic time hierarchy (small to large):**
  - 1 Nimisha = blink of an eye
  - 15 Nimisha = 1 Kashthaa
  - 30 Kashthaa = 1 Kalaa
  - 30 Kalaa = 1 Kshana
  - 6 Kshana = 1 Ghati
  - 2 Ghati = 1 Muhurta
  - 30 Muhurtas = 1 Ahoratra (day)
  - 15 days = 1 Paksha (Shukla = waxing, Krishna = waning)
  - 2 Pakshas = 1 Masa (month; 1 day for the Ancestors)
  - 2 Masas = 1 Ritu (season)
  - 3 Ritus = 1 Ayana (6 months)
  - 2 Ayanas = 1 Samvatsara (year; 1 day for the Gods)
  - 60 Samvatsaras = 1 Brihaspati Samvatsara Chakra
- **Yugas:**
  - Kali Yuga = 432,000 years
  - Dwapara Yuga = 864,000 years
  - Treta Yuga = 1,296,000 years
  - Satya Yuga = 1,728,000 years
  - 1 Maha Yuga (Chatur Yuga) = 4,320,000 years
- **Brahma's calendar:**
  - 71 Chatur Yugas = 1 Manvantara
  - 14 Manvantaras + 15 Satya Yugas = 1 Day of Brahma (1 Kalpa); same for the Night
  - 2 Kalpas = 1 Full Day of Brahma
  - 30 Days = 1 Month; 12 Months = 1 Year of Brahma
  - ~100 Years = lifespan of Brahma
- **30 Kalpas listed:** Swetha, Neelalohita, Vamadeva, Rathanthara, Rourava, Deva, Bruhath, Kandarpa, Sadhya, Eesana, Tamah, Saraswatha, Udaana, Garuda, Kaurma, Narasimha, Samana, Agneya, Soma, Maanava, Tathpuman, Vaikunta, Lakshmi, Savithri, Aghora, Vaaraah, Vairaja, Gouri, Maheswara, Pithru.
- **Brahma names/incarnations listed:** Virinchi, Padmabhu, Swayambhu, Parmeshthi (50 years), Surajyeshta (1 day), Hemagarbha (7th Manvantara), Shatanand (28th Kali Yuga), Chaturmukha, Hanuman.
- **Current Kali Yuga timeline** (per Aurobindo, *Mother India* April 2007):
  - Began at midnight 17/18 February 3102 BC
  - 5,126 years elapsed; 426,874 years left
  - Will end ~428,899 AD
- **Six Kings ruling Kali Yuga:**
  - Yudhistira Shakha: 3102 BC - 57 BC
  - Vikrama Shakha: 57 BC - 78 AD
  - Salivahana Shakha: 78 AD - 18,078 AD
  - Vijayabhinandana Shakha: 18,078 - 20,078 AD
  - Nagarjuna Shakha: 28,078 - 428,078 AD
  - Kalki Shakha: 428,078 - 428,899 AD
- **Dakshinayana** = June to January; **Uttarayana** = January to June.
- **Twelve months listed:** Chaitra, Vaishakha, Jyaishta, Ashada, Shravana, Bhadra, Asvina, Kartika, Agrahayana, Pausa, Magha, Phalguna.
- **Six seasons (Ritus):** Vasanta (spring), Grishma (summer), Varsha (monsoon), Sharada (autumn), Hemanta (pre-winter), Shishira (winter).

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Nimisha, Kashthaa, Kalaa, Kshana, Ghati, Muhurta, Ahoratra - units of time
- Paksha (Shukla / Krishna), Masa, Ritu, Ayana (Dakshinayana / Uttarayana), Samvatsara
- Yuga, Maha Yuga / Chatur Yuga, Manvantara, Kalpa
- Brihaspati Samvatsara Chakra - 60-year Jupiter cycle
- Shakha - here used as ruling era/dynasty within Kali Yuga
