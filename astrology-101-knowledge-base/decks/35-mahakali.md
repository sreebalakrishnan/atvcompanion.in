# Deck 35: Mahakali

**Source file:** `35. Mahakali.txt`
**Type:** Goddess / Element (Fire)

## Topic
Introduces Mahakali, one of the four Tridevi/Mahashakti aspects, and maps her to the **Fire** element and Fire signs. Includes a long Sri Aurobindo passage describing her qualities and a breakout-room exercise.

## Key concepts
- Mahashakti grouping shown: Mahasaraswati, Mahalakshmi, Mahakali, Maheshwari - mapped to Earth, Water, Fire, Air respectively.
- Fire signs: Aries, Leo, Sagittarius (per the diagram column headers).
- Mahakali = force, strength, intensity, immediate wrath, swift/straight/frank impulses, flame-like aspiration, tameless spirit, eagle-like vision; her love is as intense as her wrath; she shatters obstacles in a moment.
- Group exercise: collage / meal design / tourist destination / news article that embodies fiery qualities.
- Self-reflection prompts on bravery, strong feelings, anger as constructive force, big dreams, and fiery quality to grow.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Mahakali - goddess of force/strength, associated with Fire
- Mahashakti / Tridevi cluster: Mahasaraswati (Earth), Mahalakshmi (Water), Mahakali (Fire), Maheshwari (Air)
- Asura - here used metaphorically as "inner obstacles" to be burned away

## Names / references mentioned
- Sri Aurobindo, *The Mother*, p. 25 (long quoted passage)
- Tridevi: Mahasaraswati, Mahalakshmi, Mahakali, Maheshwari
