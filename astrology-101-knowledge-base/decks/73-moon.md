# Deck 73: Moon

**Source file:** `73. Moon.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Planet (Chandra)

## Topic
Companion deck to the Sun. Covers Moon astronomy, classical significations, sleep guidance (Moon governs the mind/4H/rest), the Krishna avatar association, planetary aspects and yogas (Anapha/Sunapha/Kemadruma), the "Parts of Speech" interpretive method applied to the Moon, and Moon in each Sign one-liners.

## Key concepts
- Moon astronomy: smaller than Earth but moves the tides; illuminates night; waxes/wanes; extreme temperatures; soft and beautiful; closest to Earth among Navagrahas; tiniest body but appears same size as Sun
- Significations: Emotions, the mind, habits, emotional instability, beauty, nourishment, ruler of the senses
- "Are we making something insignificant very important?" — reflective prompt about the Moon's apparent vs actual size
- Lunar timing:
  - 27-28 days for one orbit (sidereal month)
  - One day's transit = one Nakshatra
  - 2 days 6 hours per Sign
  - 29.5 days from Full Moon to Full Moon (synodic month)
- Shukla Paksha (waxing) → Purnima (Full Moon); Krishna Paksha (waning) → Amavasya (New Moon)
- Karaka chart for Moon across the 12 Houses (visual)
- Sleep:
  - Moon governs mind, emotions, 4H (rest); lunar cycle influences melatonin
  - Disturbances: mental activity, stress/trauma, screen time, animals
  - Restful sleep tips: avoid caffeine/heavy meals, cut mental activity in evenings, reduce screens, sleeping pills, look at sun in morning, wind-down ritual, consistent schedule, box breathing, Viparita Karani, dark/quiet room, no exercise before sleep
- Krishna Avatar (associated with Moon):
  - Krishna as charioteer guiding the 5 sense organs against 100 challenges
  - Pandavas = 5 sense organs (Yudhishtira/Yama, Bhima/Vayu, Arjuna/Indra, Nakula and Sahadeva/Ashwini Kumars)
  - Draupadi = Kundalini being abused by life's challenges; calls Krishna for help
  - Chariot = body / Ascendant
  - Hanuman = ambassador of Rama (the Soul), sits atop the chariot
  - Gopis = different forms of devotion (playful, joyous)
  - Radha = highest love, soul's desire to merge with Source
  - Rukmini = love and devotion combined with righteousness/duty
- Krishna reflection prompts: nurturing/protecting, detachment/equanimity, capacity to guide, compassion/empathy, unconditional love, illuminating the dark
- Classical significations:
  - Parashara: Moon = mind; windy, phlegmatic, learned, round body, auspicious looks, sweet speech, fickle-minded, lustful
  - Phala Deepika: excessive sleep, laziness, phlegmatic affliction, diarrhoea, fever, anorexia, women, jaundice, mental aberration; brings death through cholera/watery/pulmonary diseases; governs taste (with Venus); signifies left eye
  - Uttara Kalamrita: mind, mental disposition, virtue/vice, intelligence, heart, happiness, flowers, sleep, phlegm, mother, woman, Goddess Gauri worship, splendor, beauty, strength at night, west, fame, middle world, life, travel, Brahmin, water creatures, silver, pearls, soft and silk garments
- Planetary Aspects: Moon aspects only the 7th (BPHS 26:2-5)
- Yogas around the Moon (BPHS 24):
  - Anapha: planets (except Sun) in 12 from Moon → free from disease, virtuous, well-behaved, fame, comforts, dignity
  - Sunapha: planets (except Sun) in 2nd from Moon → wealthy, intelligent, self-earned prosperity, good speech, fame, independence
  - Kemadruma: no planets before or after the Moon → poor, miserable, dependent, lacks happiness/learning/support
- "Parts of Speech" framework applied to Moon:
  - Worked example: Moon in Taurus 2H → "Reflecting on my emotions supports me"
- Four interpretive intents (same as Sun deck): Prediction / Opening / Invitation / Remedy
- Moon in each Sign (one-liners): Aries — energized initiating bold changes; Taurus — needs stability/comfort to feel safe; Gemini — emotionally balanced expressing thoughts; Cancer — connected when nurturing/protecting; Leo — fulfilled when recognized/appreciated; Virgo — secure when organizing/analyzing/helping; Libra — at peace with harmony/fairness; Scorpio — alive in deep emotional transformation; Sagittarius — free when exploring/learning/expanding; Capricorn — in control with long-term goals; Aquarius — myself with unconventional ideas; Pisces — connected when trusting intuition/emotions
