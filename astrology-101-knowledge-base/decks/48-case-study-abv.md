# Deck 48: Case Study - ABV (Atal Bihari Vajpayee)

**Source file:** `48. Case Study - ABV.pdf`
**Type:** Case Study

## Topic
Group exercise applying foundational chart-reading techniques to the birth chart of Atal Bihari Vajpayee, framed against his decision to authorise India's 1998 Pokhran nuclear tests (Operation Shakti).

## Key concepts
- Background context: Vajpayee as PM took the strategic call to proceed with nuclear tests; May 1998 Pokhran tests declared India a nuclear weapons state; international sanctions followed but long-term geopolitical positioning improved.
- Group exercise (2-3 learners): build Vajpayee's Vedic chart and answer:
  - Longitude of each Graha; which are retrograde; which sign each is in.
  - Which Grahas are in own/exalted/debilitated/Moola Trikona signs.
  - How many days after birth Mercury and Jupiter become conjunct.
  - Which signs have no Grahas.
  - Whether he was born during an eclipse; waxing or waning Moon.
  - Dispositor and Final Dispositor of each Graha.
  - Compound relationship of the Sun with all other Grahas.
  - Duality, Modality, Element of each Graha; is there a Signature; interpret predominant Duality/Modality/Element.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- (recap) Graha, Moola Trikona, Dispositor, Compound Relationship, Signature.
