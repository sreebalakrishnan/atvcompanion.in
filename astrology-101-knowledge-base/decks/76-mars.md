# Deck 76: Mars

**Source file:** `76. Mars.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Planet (Sanskrit)

## Topic
Deck on Mars (Mangala/Kuja): astronomy-to-meaning mappings, the Narasimha Avatar tie-in, classical significations, special aspects, key Mars yogas, the Kuja Dosha (Manglik) framework with cancellations, and Mars-in-Sign one-liners.

## Key concepts
- Astronomy → meaning mappings:
  - Small planet but tallest volcano and deepest canyon → Action
  - Thin atmosphere, harsh climate → Reckless
  - Red colour → Anger, blood, fire
  - Fast rotation period (similar to Earth) → Strength
  - Asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter → Battle
  - Has asteroids in its own orbit → Commander
  - Throws asteroids at Earth → Destruction
- Narasimha Avatar association: fierce, aggressive form of Vishnu who appeared to destroy evil and protect His devotee from a tyrant of a father
- Reflection prompts:
  - Has aggressive energy been constructive and/or destructive in your life? How can you channel it positively?
  - Have you stood up for someone/something you believed in despite opposition?
  - Have you witnessed injustice — how did you react and address it?
- Classical significations:
  - Parashara: strength; blood-red eyes; fickle-minded; liberal; bilious; given to anger; thin waist and physique
  - Phala Deepika: courage, disease, characteristic qualities, younger brothers, lands, enemies, blood relations; cook, arms-bearer, goldsmith, ram, cock, jackal, monkey, vulture, thief; products from Earth, brothers, cruelty, battle, daring acts, kitchen, fire, gold, weapons, energy, falsehood, prowess, sin, commencing an army, wounds
  - Uttara Kalamrita: extensive list — valor, prowess, independence, persistency, carrying arms, loss of virility, thief, battle, hostility, brother, enemy's strength, victory over enemy, fame, kingship, army commander, generosity, firmness, deep red colour, land, sacred places, anger, quadruped, fire, painful urination, controversy, scandal, bile, sky, sight, wound, fracture, pain, Sataghni, sword, spear, axe, archery, hatchet, forester, minister, village headman, prayer to Subrahmanya, youth, pungent, Durbar Hall, earthen pots, eating meat, strong at end of night, jewel, gold, goldsmith, tin, summer season, square, copper, sound of trumpet, facing South, Sama Veda, controlling wild animals, serpent, fickle-mindedness, blood, coagulation of blood
  - Source citation: Parashara's Light 9.01
- Planetary aspects: Mars aspects 4th, 7th and 8th (special aspects); however Mars does not have a Special Aspect on Jupiter. Source: BPHS 26:2-5
- Key Mars yogas:
  - **Ruchaka Yoga**: Mars in own, exalted or Moolatrikona Sign and in a Kendra from Ascendant → wealth through courageous deeds, valour, conquers enemies, arrogance, fame, commander of an army, success in ventures (Phaladeepika 6.2)
  - **Chandra Mangala Yoga**: Moon and Mars 1 or 7 from each other → dealer in hammer, ploughs, rough instruments, earthen pots (Phaladeepika 18.2); financial wealth/earnings through trade (BV Raman, Combination 6)
  - **Graha Yuddha** (planetary war): within 1 degree in any Sign — does not apply to Sun and Moon. Planet with lesser longitude wins; if equal, higher declination wins. Winner gains strength, loser becomes weak (BPHS 11.16)
- Kuja Dosha / Manglik:
  - BPHS 80.47: Mars in 12th, 4th, 7th or 8th from Lagna without aspect by, or association with, any benefic
  - Generally used: Mars in 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 12 from Ascendant, Moon or Venus
  - Effects: tension, impulsiveness, dominance — better addressed before emotional commitments
  - Cancellations: (1) both partners have similar dosha; (2) Mars in own, exalted or friendly Sign; (3) aspect from a benefic, or benefic in 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 12; (4) Ascendant Lord is strong; (5) Ascendant is Aries, Cancer, Leo or Scorpio
- Mars in each Sign (one-liners using duality/modality/element framing):
  - Aries — act boldly and initiate with courage
  - Taurus — patiently walk towards my goals
  - Gemini — act dynamically and adapt my words
  - Cancer — act protectively and nurture my emotions
  - Leo — act passionately and persevere with confidence
  - Virgo — act patiently and improve through discipline
  - Libra — act from clarity and assert my freedom
  - Scorpio — act consistently for my inner truth
  - Sagittarius — act spontaneously in power
  - Capricorn — act strategically and build with discipline
  - Aquarius — act courageously and focus on play
  - Pisces — act intuitively and softly collaborate

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Mangala / Kuja — Mars
- Ruchaka Yoga — Pancha Mahapurusha yoga for Mars in own/exalted/MT in Kendra
- Chandra Mangala Yoga — Moon-Mars 1/7 axis combination
- Graha Yuddha — planetary war (within 1 degree)
- Kuja Dosha / Manglik — Mars affliction in marriage houses
- Moolatrikona (MT) — sign of own root strength
- Kendra — angular house (1, 4, 7, 10)

## Notes
Parallel structure to the Sun (#72) and Moon (#73) decks: astronomy → avatar → reflections → classical significations → aspects → yogas → Sign one-liners. Adds two material specific to Mars: (a) the Kuja Dosha / Manglik framework with explicit cancellation rules, and (b) Graha Yuddha (planetary war) mechanics. Mars's three special aspects (4/7/8) are the first non-7th aspects introduced after Sun and Moon.
