# Deck 12: Planet in Sign & Nakshatra

**Source file:** `12. Planet in Sign & Nakshatra.txt`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals

## Topic
Introduces the zodiac (Rasi) and lunar mansions (Nakshatras) as two parallel divisions of the ecliptic. Establishes the math (30° per sign, 13°20' per nakshatra) and lists Sanskrit/English names plus the bright stars associated with each nakshatra.

## Key concepts
- Two ways to divide the ecliptic (360° circle):
  - 12 Zodiacal Signs (Rasi) → 30° each
  - 27 Nakshatras (Lunar Mansions) → 13°20' each
- One sign holds ~2.25 nakshatras; 4.5 in 2 signs; 6.75 in 3; 9 in 4 (i.e., every 4 signs hold exactly 9 nakshatras)
- Sign listing with English/Sanskrit names and a letter code (A-L starting from Aries):
  - Aries / Mesha (A), Taurus / Vrishabha (B), Gemini / Mithuna (C), Cancer / Karkataka (D), Leo / Simha (E), Virgo / Kanya (F), Libra / Tula (G), Scorpio / Vrischik (H), Sagittarius / Dhanus (I), Capricorn / Makara (J), Aquarius / Kumbha (K), Pisces / Meena (L)
- 27 Nakshatras with their associated stars (selected): Ashwini-Beta Arietis, Bharani-35/39/41 Arietis, Krittika-Pleiades (Alcyone), Rohini-Aldebaran, Mrigashirsha-Lambda Orionis, Ardra-Betelgeuse, Punarvasu-Castor, Pushya-Gamma Cancri, Ashlesha-Hydrae, Magha-Regulus, Purva Phalguni-Delta Leonis, Uttara Phalguni-Denebola, Hasta-Delta Corvi, Chitra-Spica, Swati-Arcturus, Vishakha-Alpha Librae, Anuradha-Beta/Delta/Pi Scorpii, Jyeshtha-Antares, Mula-Lambda Scorpii, Purva Ashadha-Delta Sagittarii, Uttara Ashadha-Sigma Sagittarii, Shravana-Altair, Dhanishta-Delphinus, Shatabhisha-Gamma Aquarii, Purva Bhadrapada-Alpha Pegasi, Uttara Bhadrapada-Gamma Pegasi, Revati-Zeta Piscium
- Stars are named by Greek letters in order of brightness within a constellation (Alpha = brightest)
- Greek alphabet listed in full (alpha through omega) for reference

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Rasi — zodiacal sign
- Nakshatra — lunar mansion
- 12 sign Sanskrit names (Mesha, Vrishabha, Mithuna, Karkataka, Simha, Kanya, Tula, Vrischik, Dhanus, Makara, Kumbha, Meena)
- All 27 nakshatra names
