# Deck 57: Intro to Houses

**Source file:** `57. Intro to Houses.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Houses

## Topic
Introduces the 12 Houses (Bhavas) by progressively layering the life areas each house represents, building up the wheel from spatial cues ("My work" at top, "My home" at bottom) to a fully labelled 1H-12H map.

## Key concepts
- Sign chart vs. House chart (recap):
  - Sign chart starts at 0 deg Aries; 12 signs of 30 deg; defined by Duality, Modality, Element.
  - House chart starts at the Ascendant; 12 houses of approximately 30 deg; each House denotes different areas of life.
  - Both are necessary for chart interpretation.
- Houses introduced as life areas (built up across slides):
  - 1H — Myself
  - 2H — Resources, Family, My support
  - 3H — My hobbies, siblings, effort
  - 4H — My home, mother, emotions, fixed assets
  - 5H — My creations, children, romance, entertainment
  - 6H — My health, competitors, diseases, uncle
  - 7H — My commitments, spouse, business
  - 8H — My challenges, obstacles and hurdles, longevity
  - 9H — My learning, teacher, higher education, long-distance travel
  - 10H — Government, Father, Boss, My work
  - 11H — Awards, Recognition, Friends, My status
  - 12H — Expenses, Sub-conscious, My morning practice

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Bhava — house (implicit; "Houses" used throughout).
- Lagna / Ascendant — implied as 1H starting point.
