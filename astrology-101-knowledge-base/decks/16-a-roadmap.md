# Deck 16: A Roadmap

**Source file:** `16. A Roadmap.txt`
**Type:** Logistics / Learning Path

## Topic
Defines the four-stage progression for learners on the path to reading birth charts: Foundations, Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced. Frames chart reading as a responsibility and emphasizes the question "how can I help?"

## Key concepts
- Reading someone else's chart is a responsibility; readiness is self-evident.
- Four-stage roadmap: Foundations to Beginner to Intermediate to Advanced.
- Foundations: use your own birth chart as a mirror; no interpretation of others' charts; observe the first year; maintain a journal of insights and events shared with the teacher.
- Beginner: build technical skills; deepen astrology principles; interpret a mentor's birth chart; receive feedback after each session; mentor new joiners. Goal: 3 readings for 1 mentor.
- Intermediate: read charts with guidance; for each chart discuss with mentor, complete the reading, reflect/review with mentor; mentor new joiners. Goal: 7-10 clients.
- Advanced: read charts independently; monthly reflections with peers; keep a journal of learnings; apply to your own chart; mentor new joiners. Duration: 6-7 months.
