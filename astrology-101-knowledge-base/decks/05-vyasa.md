# Deck 5: Vyasa

**Source file:** `5. Vyasa.txt`
**Type:** Mythology / Lineage

## Topic
Story of Veda Vyasa — birth, role as compiler of the Vedas, role in the Mahabharata, and his place in the lineage of Vedic Astrology sages.

## Key concepts
- Parashara (father) + Satyavati (mother) → Vyasa
- Born on Full Moon in Sagittarius
- Born on island in Yamuna river near Kalpi → also called Krishna Dwaipayana ("dark one of the island")
- Initiated by Vasudeva
- Compiled and divided the Vedas → hence "Veda Vyasa"
- Through niyoga with Ambika, Ambalika, Parishrami: fathered Dhritarashtra, Pandu, Vidura → ancestors of 101 Kauravas, 5 Pandavas, Anasava
- "Splitting the embryo" reference (Gandhari's 100 sons)
- Witnessed Mahabharata; gave divine sight to Sanjaya
- Connections to Brahma and Ganesha (Ganesha as scribe of Mahabharata)
- Associated with Badrinath; meeting with Adi Shankara
- Listed as one of the 18 Sages of Vedic Astrology

## The 18 Sages of Vedic Astrology
Surya, Brahma-Pitamaha, Vyasa, Vashistha, Kashyapa, Atri, Parashara, Narada, Garga, Manu, Romasa, Chyavana, Bhrigu, Mareechi, Angiras, Poulasa, Yavana, Saunaka
