# Deck 39: Maheshwari

**Source file:** `39. Maheshwari.txt`
**Type:** Goddess / Element (Air)

## Topic
Introduces Maheshwari, the Tridevi/Mahashakti aspect mapped to the **Air** element. Includes a long Sri Aurobindo passage on her qualities and a breakout-room exercise applied to all four elements.

## Key concepts
- Mahashakti grouping: Mahasaraswati (Earth), Mahalakshmi (Water), Mahakali (Fire), **Maheshwari (Air)**.
- Air signs: Gemini, Libra, Aquarius (per the columns).
- Maheshwari = sublimated wisdom above the thinking mind; opens to supramental infinities and cosmic vastness; comprehends all beings, laws, times; impartial, follows the decrees of the Supreme.
- She gives wisdom to the wise, includes those with vision in her counsels, and leads even the ignorant according to their state. Compassion does not blind her wisdom; Truth and knowledge are her concern.
- Group activation exercise (each element): find a corporate brand / cartoon character / childhood incident / personal photo that reflects each element.
- Air self-reflection prompts: where you feel free, when you last used wisdom for others, scattered/restless states, open-mindedness, "seeing the big picture", quality to grow this month.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Maheshwari - goddess of wisdom, mapped to Air
- Supramental - Aurobindo's term for a level of consciousness above mind
- (Tridevi cluster as before: Mahasaraswati, Mahalakshmi, Mahakali, Maheshwari)

## Names / references mentioned
- Sri Aurobindo, *The Mother*, p. 24 (long quoted passage)
