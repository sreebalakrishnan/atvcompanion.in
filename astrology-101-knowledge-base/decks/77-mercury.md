# Deck 77: Mercury

**Source file:** `77. Mercury.pdf`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals / Planet (Sanskrit)

## Topic
Deck on Mercury (Budha): astronomy-to-meaning mappings, the Buddha Avatar tie-in, classical significations, a Stellarium exercise, the eight apparent motion states with retrograde durations by planet, Mercury yogas, and Mercury-in-Sign one-liners.

## Key concepts
- Astronomy → meaning mappings:
  - Goes around the Sun every 88 days; fastest moving planet → Quick
  - Extreme temperatures → Dual natured, neutral
  - Rotates 2x every 3 orbits → Unique, intellect, analysis
  - Very large iron core → Nervous system
  - Permanent ice at the poles → Emotions not seen
- Buddha Avatar association — reflection prompts:
  - Intelligence: Am I looking for an intellectual understanding, or wisdom that transforms me?
  - Communication: Do my words bring clarity, or cause more harm/confusion?
  - The Middle Path: How balanced is my lifestyle? Do I go to extremes, or is my life in balance?
  - Equanimity and detachment: Am I attached to material possessions/desires? How can I focus on what truly matters?
- Classical significations:
  - Parashara: speech, skin; attractive physique; capacity to use words with many meanings; fond of jokes; mix of all three humours
  - Phala Deepika: mental disease, vocal organs, eye/throat/nose disease, fever, tridoshic (Vata/Pitta/Kapha) disorders, poison, skin disease, anaemia, bad dreams, itches/scab/Psoriasis, accidents from fire, violence, imprisonment, harm from evil demons in Gandharva abodes; horses; causes death through anaemia/giddiness; governs smell (nose)
  - Uttara Kalamrita: speech, courteous speech, writing, education, scholar, wise lectures, Vedanta philosophy, mathematics, grammar, Telugu language, astrology, mantras, amulets, legends of past, green colour, garden, beautiful house, treasury, infantry, palatial building, sculpture, Brahmin, devotion, pilgrimage, temple, worship of Vishnu, trading, horses, ornaments, new garment, impartiality, tranquility, modesty, eunuch, facing North, wet, bell metal, renunciation, child, crooked look, heaven, fear, skin, navel, neck, humorous temperament, dancing, powerful in morning, Hemanta Ritu, sharpening stone, family prosperity, Sudra, bird, after-born, the Directions, Atharva Veda, tower, virile, benefic, Northwest quarter, well, doctor, tester of precious stones, paternal relation, maternal grandfather, maternal uncle
- Stellarium exercise:
  1. Open Stellarium
  2. Adjust date, time and place to current
  3. Pause time
  4. Turn off atmosphere
  5. Find Mercury
  6. Turn on Constellations (lines, labels and art)
  7. Switch to Indian Vedic Sky Culture
  8. Tick Zodiac and Lunar Stations
  9. Identify Mercury's Sign and Nakshatra
  10. Repeat for birth date, time and place
- Apparent motions (eight states):
  - Sama — usual speed
  - Manda — slowing down
  - Mandotar — slowest motion
  - Vikala — stationary
  - Vakra — appears retrograde (moving back)
  - Char — slightly faster than usual
  - Atichar — enters next sign in direct motion
  - Anuvakra — enters previous sign while retrograde
  - Source: BPHS Ch. 29, Verses 21-23
- Retrograde durations:
  - Sun and Moon — never retrograde
  - Mercury — 3 weeks, 3-4 times a year
  - Venus — 6 weeks, every 18 months
  - Mars — 2-3 months, every 2 years
  - Jupiter — 4 months, once a year
  - Saturn — 4-5 months, once a year
  - Uranus / Neptune — 5 months, once a year
  - Pluto — 5-6 months, once a year
  - Rahu and Ketu — always retrograde
- Planetary aspects: Mercury aspects only the 7th (implied by absence of special aspect)
- Mercury yogas:
  - **Bhadra Yoga**: Mercury in own, exalted or MT and in a Kendra → intelligent, learned, eloquent, pure in conduct (BPHS 76.6-7)
  - **Budha Aditya Yoga**: Sun and Mercury together in any Sign → intelligent, endowed with discrimination (BPHS 15.1-5; not called Budha Aditya Yoga by Parashara)
  - **Lakshmi Narayana Yoga**: Venus and Mercury together in any Sign → wealth, prosperity, financial success; intellect, business and persuasive skills; success in luxury, arts, finance, entrepreneurship; balanced material and spiritual growth (Source: unknown)
- Mercury in each Sign (one-liners):

  - Gemini — think courageously in responding to intellectual topics
  - Cancer — think creatively in leading intense spaces

  - Virgo — think peacefully for collaborating in practical matters
  - Libra — think enthusiastically in asserting my freedom
  - Scorpio — think compassionately in persevering to the truth
  - Sagittarius — think optimistically to collaborate for transformation
  - Capricorn — think calmly in setting practical goals
  - Aquarius — think innovatively to sustain my freedom
  - Pisces — think peacefully while communicating my emotions
