# Deck 43: Planetary Cabinet

**Source file:** `43. Planetary Cabinet.txt`
**Type:** Astrology fundamentals

## Topic
Introduces the "Planetary Cabinet" model: each planet (graha) has a **Ruler / Pati** sign, a **Moola Trikona** (its "office"), and **Exaltation / Debilitation** (Uccha / Neecha) signs. The deck lays this out across three full zodiac diagrams.

## Key concepts
- **Rulership (Ruler / Pati):**
  - Sun rules Leo
  - Moon rules Cancer
  - Mercury rules Gemini and Virgo (with Rahu also rules Virgo)
  - Venus rules Taurus and Libra
  - Mars rules Aries and Scorpio (with Ketu also rules Scorpio)
  - Jupiter rules Sagittarius and Pisces (with Ketu also rules Pisces)
  - Saturn rules Capricorn and Aquarius (with Rahu also rules Aquarius)
- **Moola Trikona ("Office of the Planet" - where it works best):**
  - Sun works in Leo
  - Moon works in Taurus
  - Mercury works in Virgo
  - Venus works in Libra
  - Mars works in Aries
  - Jupiter works in Sagittarius (with Ketu also in Sagittarius)
  - Saturn works in Aquarius
  - Rahu works in Gemini
- **Exaltation (Uccha) and Debilitation (Neecha):**
  - Sun: exalts Aries, debilitates Libra
  - Moon: exalts Taurus, debilitates Scorpio
  - Mercury: exalts Virgo, debilitates Pisces
  - Venus: exalts Pisces, debilitates Virgo
  - Mars: exalts Capricorn, debilitates Cancer
  - Jupiter: exalts Cancer, debilitates Capricorn
  - Saturn: exalts Libra, debilitates Aries
  - Rahu: exalts Taurus, debilitates Scorpio
  - Ketu: exalts Scorpio, debilitates Taurus
- The "Building Blocks of the Signs" recap is included at the end (Fire/Earth/Air/Water + Cardinal/Fixed/Mutable + Yang/Yin grids).

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Graha - planet
- Pati - ruler / lord (of a sign)
- Moola Trikona - the planet's "office" or most effective working sign (between own house and exaltation)
- Uccha - exaltation
- Neecha - debilitation
- Dispositor - the ruler of the sign a planet sits in (concept used in deck 45)
