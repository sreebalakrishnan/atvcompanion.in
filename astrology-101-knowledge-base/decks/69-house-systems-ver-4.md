## Topic
Fourth iteration of the House Systems deck. Carries forward all material from version 3 and adds: a Venus-in-Sagittarius worked example for Sripati Paddhathi, a clear summary slide on the geometry, and a homework block instructing students to compute their own Sripati House chart.

## Key concepts
- Full Sign + Equal House (carried over)
- Sripati Paddhathi (carried over) with a new Venus example:
  - Venus in Sagittarius at 5 deg 12' 07" = 245 deg 12' 07"
  - In Sign chart: Sagittarius is 11th Sign
  - In Bhava chart: falls in 10th House (cusps 249.5 to 278.5 region)
- Geometry summary slide:
  - Ascendant = intersection of ecliptic with horizon in the East
  - M.C. (10th cusp) = intersection of ecliptic with local meridian (longitude)
  - Horizon and longitude intersect ecliptic at different angles
  - Ecliptic tilted 23.4 deg relative to celestial equator
  - As you move away from the equator, the angle between ecliptic and horizon changes, MC shifts relative to Ascendant
- Homework / "Try it out":
  - Use https://www.onlinejyotish.com/free-astrology/vedic-horoscope.php for 10th cusp longitude
  - Add 180 deg to Ascendant for Descendant (7th cusp)
  - Add 180 deg to 10th cusp for IC (4th cusp)
  - Add 180 deg to cusps 8-12 for cusps 2-6
  - Build the House chart for own birth chart and a famous personality
- Additional reading: http://www.exeterastrologygroup.org.uk/2015/01/the-definition-of-midheaven_18.html
