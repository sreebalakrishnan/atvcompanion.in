# Deck 52: Astronomy of Houses

**Source file:** `52. Astronomy of Houses.pdf`
**Type:** Astronomy / Houses

## Topic
Bridges astronomy to the concept of Houses (Bhavas). Introduces the four cardinal points of the local sky (Ascendant, Descendant, Medium Coeli, Imum Coeli), distinguishes a Sign chart from a House chart, and references the auspicious chart of Sri Rama from the Valmiki Ramayana.

## Key concepts
- Local sky frame: Sky above the horizon vs. sky below; East and West points.
- Four key points of the celestial sphere relative to the observer:
  - Ascendant (Asc) = 1st house cusp, eastern horizon.
  - Descendant (Desc) = 7th house cusp, western horizon.
  - Medium Coeli (MC) = 10th house cusp, top of the sky.
  - Imum Coeli (IC) = 4th house cusp, bottom (below horizon).
- Houses 1 to 12 mapped onto the wheel relative to the horizon and meridian, demonstrated with Cancer rising:
  - 1H (Asc) = Cancer, 2H = Leo, 3H = Virgo, 4H (IC) = Libra, ... 7H (Desc) = Capricorn, ... 10H (MC) = Aries, ... 12H = Gemini.
- Sign chart vs. House chart:
  - Sign chart: starts from 0 deg Aries; 12 signs of 30 deg each.
  - House chart: starts from the Ascendant; 12 houses (each approximately 30 deg).
  - Both are used together in Astrology.
- Sri Rama's chart (per Valmiki Ramayana 1:18:8-9, in Sanskrit and transliteration):
  - Twelfth month after the yajna, ninth tithi of Chaitra month, nakshatra Punarvasu, five planets exalted, Moon and Jupiter conjunct on the horizon in Cancer (Cancer Lagna). Kausalya gave birth to Rama.
- Homework: build your own birth chart on deva.guru or similar; compare South vs North Indian styles; note which planet is in which sign and which house.

## Sanskrit/technical terms introduced
- Bhava — house.
- Lagna — Ascendant.
- Ascendant / Descendant.
- Medium Coeli (MC) / Imum Coeli (IC) — Latin terms for the meridian points.
- Tithi — lunar day.
- Nakshatra — lunar mansion (Punarvasu in the example).
