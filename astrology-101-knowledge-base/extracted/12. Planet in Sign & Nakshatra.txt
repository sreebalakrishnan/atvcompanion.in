The math

Circle is 360o

Divide into 12 segments, called the Zodiacal Signs (Rasi)

360 / 12 = 30o each Sign (pronounced as 30 degrees)



Divide into 27 segments, called the Lunar Mansions (Nakshatra)

360 / 27 = 13o 20I for each Nakshatra (pronounced as “13 degrees 20 minutes”)

The math

A Sign is 30o

A Nakshatra is 13o 20I

So, one Sign can hold a little more than 2 Nakshatras

30O / 13o 20I

30 / 13.33333

2.25 Nakshatras in 1 Sign
4.5 Nakshatras in 2 Signs
6.75 Nakshatras in 3 Signs
9 Nakshatras in 4 Signs
