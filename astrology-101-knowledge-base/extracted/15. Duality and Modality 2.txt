The Dualities

1. How comfortable am I in receiving support (or help) from others?
2. What motivates me – is it inner alignment or external validation?
3. In which area of my life, am I being asked to slow down a little?
4. Where / when do I hold back from bold decisions?
5. Do I rest, reflect, and allow myself to be still?
6. Am I expressing my emotions, or supressing them?
7. What is my relationship with “productivity”
8. Is my sensitivity a strength or a burden?
9. How do I handle competition?
10. Do I allow myself to become vulnerable?
11. Where do I push too hard?
12. Am I withdrawing from engaging with the world?

The Modalities

1. When do I feel most alive - when starting, persevering, or completing something?
2. When faced with a new opportunity, do I tend to act quickly or wait?
3. Do I struggle with sticking to one thing?
4. What does “commitment” mean to me?
5. How do I respond when someone challenges my beliefs?
6. Am I truly flexible, or do I avoid commitment?
7. Do I often find myself taking the lead in group situations?
8. Do I adapt to unexpected changes?
9. Do I value stability and status quo over growth?
10. In what situations do I become resistant to change?
11. Do I persist even when something no longer serves me?
12. Am I more motivated by beginnings or by the results?
13. Do I lose interest once the initial excitement wears off?
14. Do I like juggling multiple things? Why is that?
