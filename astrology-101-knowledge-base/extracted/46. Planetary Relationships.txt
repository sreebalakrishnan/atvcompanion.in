Natural Relationships
To find the natural relationship b/w Planet 1 and Planet 2:

1. Note the Moola Trikona sign of Planet 1

2. Find the Signs owned by Planet 2

3. Does Planet 1 exalt in those Signs? If yes, then “Friend”

4. Are those Signs 2, 4, 5, 8, 9 or 12 from Moola Trikona of
   Planet 1? If yes, then “Friend” else “Enemy”

5. If a relationship is “Friend” for one Sign & “Enemy” to
   another, then it is “Neutral”

Source: BPHS 3:55
