30° ÷ 15° = 2 hours
12:00 noon + 2 hours = 2:00 PM                                         5. If it is 10:00 PM at 120°E, what is the time at 60°W?

45°W to 15°W = difference of 30°                                       60°W is west of 120°E → subtract 12 hours
30° ÷ 15° = 2 hours                                                    10:00 PM – 12 hours = 10:00 AM
