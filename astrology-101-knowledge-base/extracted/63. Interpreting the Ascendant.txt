Interpreting the Ascendant
Using your birth chart,

    1.   Write down the factual statement of the Ascendant, and the lord of the Ascendant. Do not
         consider aspects at this stage.
    2.   Note a word or two for each part of the factual statement, from the respective flashcards.
    3.   Next, write a few short sentences interpreting the factual statement.

Upload your answers as a Word doc (not as a pdf) in the Case Studies folder. An example is given
below.



Factual statements
   • My Ascendant is Cancer.
   • The lord of the Ascendant is the Moon.
   • Moon is placed in Pisces.
   • Pisces is the 9th House.



Words from the flashcards
   A. Ascendant = identity
   B. Ascendant Sign Cancer = yin, cardinal, water
       • Yin = introvert
       • Cardinal = leader
       • Water = emotions
   C. Ascendant Lord Moon = Krishna Avatar
   D. Ascendant Lord in Pisces = yin, mutable, water
       • Yin = introvert
       • Mutable = adpatable
       • Water = emotions
   E. Pisces is 9th House = higher education



Sentences interpreting the factual statement
   1. Sentences connecting A & B
       • I am emotional
       • I am an introvert
       • I am a leader

    2.   Sentences connecting C & D
         • I am adaptable

    3.   Sentences connecting C & E
         • My identity is linked to my higher education
         • I guide others in higher education
         • I nurture and protect my feelings
