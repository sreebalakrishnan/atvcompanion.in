Time for each degree

Earth makes 1 rotation every 24 hours
1 rotation is 360 degrees
360 degrees of arc in 24 hours
Hence, the Earth rotates 15 degrees of arc every hour (i.e. 360/24)
15 degrees of arc every 60 minutes
Or 1 degree of arc every 4 minutes (i.e. 60/15)
