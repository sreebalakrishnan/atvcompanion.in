Degrees

Circle has 3600 (pronounced as 360 degrees)
Each degree has 60l (pronounced as 60 minutes)
Each minute has 60ll (pronounced as 60 seconds)
Different from hours : minutes : seconds (hh:mm:ss)


Twelve spokes, one wheel, navels three.
Who can comprehend this?
On it are placed together
three hundred and sixty like pegs.
They shake not in the least.

— Rigveda 1.164.48
