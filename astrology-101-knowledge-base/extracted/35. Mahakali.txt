Mahakali

“Force and strength are her peculiar power. There is in her an
overwhelming intensity, a mighty passion of force to achieve, a
divine violence rushing to shatter every limit and obstacle. She
never shrinks from the battle. Her wrath is immediate. The
impulses that are swift and straight and frank, the aspiration that
mounts in flame are the motion of Mahakali. Her spirit is tameless,
her vision and will are high and far-reaching like the flight of an
eagle. Her love is as intense as her wrath and she has a deep and
passionate kindness. When she is allowed to intervene in her
strength, then in one moment are broken the obstacles. Nothing
can satisfy her that falls short of the supreme ecstasies, the
highest heights, the noblest aims, the largest vistas. Therefore with
her is the victorious force of the Divine and it is by grace of her fire
and passion and speed if the great achievement can be done now
than hereafter”

Sri Aurobindo, in “The Mother”, pg 25

Fire

When have you felt brave like a warrior? What did you learn about
your own strength?

Think of a time you felt deeply about something. How did that
feel? What “asuras” (inner obstacles) did you burn away in the
process?

Did you ever feel so full of energy that you wanted to change
everything? What positive change could you start today?

Strong feelings can turn into anger sometimes. Have you ever used
your anger to do something good?

What is your big dream in life? What’s one small step you can take
toward it today?

Which fiery quality do you want to grow in yourself this month? Is
there something around you that reminds you of this quality?
