Aspects

Signs and Planets look at different
regions in space

By the mere act of looking, it transfers
its energy to that region

Anything in that region in space gets
the energy of the aspect

Aspects can be benefic or malefic
depending on what is aspecting that
region.

Graha Dhrishti / Planetary Aspects

Planets aspect a region in space, and everything in that region
To determine whether an aspect exists, we need to count

Count from the Aspecting Planet
Count from the Sign that the Aspecting Planet is placed in
This is 1

Count Signs in the clockwise direction

Count to the Sign or Planet in question.

Orb of influence

Intensity of the Aspect is not uniformly distributed

The closer to the exact degree, the more intense the aspect

Orb of influence is usually ±7.5° but changes with software

Exact degree           Full aspect
± 0° to 2.5°           Moderate        3/4th aspect
± 2.5° to 5°           Medium          ½ aspect
± 5° to 7.5°           Weakest         1/4th aspect




Source: BPHS, 26:2-5
