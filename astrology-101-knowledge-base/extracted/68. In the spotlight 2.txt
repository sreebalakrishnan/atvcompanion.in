In the spotlight
As astrologers, it’s important not only to
understand the concepts, but also to be able to
share our understanding with others.

So I’d like you to create a short video, around 5
minutes long, interpreting your ascendant to me!

Use the recent decks uploaded on Teams, to get
the basics of what the Ascendant means, as well
as the homework you have shared with me on
Google Drive.

The goal is to get comfortable talking in front of the
camera, because as astrologers, we need to be
able to communicate clearly and effectively. Don’t
worry about making it perfect - just focus on
explaining in your own words.
On Sunday 8th March, in the class timeslot:
1. During regular class hours, pair up with your partner
   (if you have one), discuss what the Ascendant
   means (astronomically and astrologically).
2. Individually, use the camera on your cell phone to
   video record yourself.
3. Practice explaining what the Ascendant means
   (astronomically), and then interpret it in your chart.
4. Practice once, and then hit record! Stammering, bad
   hairstyle, poor lighting, disturbing background, noisy
   environment are all ok!
5. Intention of this exercise is to get comfortable being
   in the spotlight, and have fun talking about
   Astrology!
