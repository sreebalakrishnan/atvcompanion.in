Don’t try to remember the details. Look for themes
that are coming up in your life. Regard these stories
as mirrors

Contradictions are okay. There will be many
versions of a story or many interpretations. It’s like
looking at the same tree from different directions

Pick a thread. Go deep, make it personal. See where
it leads you...

Learn in layers. Revisit a topic - say, Saturn - and
you’ll see something new. These topics will reveal
something new each time you visit them..

This is not a subject that will give you a neat closure.
Can you just sit and be ok with knowing or not-
knowing.. that’s when the real magic will happen.
