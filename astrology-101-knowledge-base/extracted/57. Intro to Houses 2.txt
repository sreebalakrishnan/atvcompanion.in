Houses
Sign chart vs. House chart

Sign chart starts from 0o Aries
There are 12 Signs. In Astrology, each Sign is 30o
Each Sign has different traits
The building blocks of the Signs are Duality, Modality and Element


House chart starts from the Ascendant
There are 12 Houses. Each House is approximately 30o
Each House denotes different areas of life


Both are necessary for chart interpretation
