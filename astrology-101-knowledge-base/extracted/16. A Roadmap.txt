Foundations

Focus: Use your birth chart as a mirror

You begin with your own birth chart

No interpretation of others’ charts

Observe how the first year plays out in
your life

Goal: Maintain a journal of insights and
events, and share this with me

Deepen Astrology principles

Interpret a mentor’s birth chart

Receive feedback after each session

Mentor new joiners

Goal: 3 readings for 1 mentor

   Reflect and review with your mentor

Mentor new joiners

Goal: 7-10 clients
