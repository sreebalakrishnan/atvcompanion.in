Maheshwari

“Sits above the thinking mind and will and sublimates them into
wisdom. She is the mighty and wise, opens us to the supramental
infinities and the cosmic vastness, to a treasure-house of
miraculous knowledge, to the measureless movement of the
Mother’s eternal forces. Nothing can move her because all
wisdom is in her; nothing is hidden from her that she chooses to
know; she comprehends all things and all beings and their nature
and what moves them and the law of the world and its times and
how all was and is and must be. Partiality she has none, but she
follows the decrees of the Supreme. To the wise she gives a
greater and more luminous wisdom; those that have vision she
admits to her counsels; the ignorant and foolish she leads
according to their blindness. For she is above all, bound by
nothing, attached to nothing in the universe. But her compassion
does not blind her wisdom or turn her action from the course
decreed; for the Truth of things is her one concern, knowledge her
centre of power.”

Sri Aurobindo, in “The Mother”, pg 24

clarity?
