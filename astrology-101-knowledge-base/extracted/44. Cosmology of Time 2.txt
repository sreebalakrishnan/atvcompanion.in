   yadā yadā hi dharmasya glānir bhavati bhārata
abhyutthānam adharmasya tadātmānam srijāmyaham

Whenever there is a fall in righteousness, Oh descendant
 of Bharata, and a rise in unrighteousness, I manifest.

  paritrānnāya sādhūnām vināśhāya cha dushkritām
 dharma-sansthāpanārthāya sambhavāmi yuge yuge

    To protect the righteous, to destroy the wicked,
 and to reestablish the Dharma, I appear, age after age.

                 Bhagawad Gita, 2.12 – 2.13
                 by Swami Mukundananda
