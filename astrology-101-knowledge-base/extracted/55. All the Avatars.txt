Dwapura Yuga
Problem about greed and moral confusion
Good and evil in the same family, strategic, no rigid rules, context is
key, dharma is debated. Fully developed humans.


Kali Yuga
Problems with superficial nature, low inner values
Good and evil in the same person, no heavy metaphysics, no misuse
of ritual or violence. Pre frontal cortex, latest in human evolution.
