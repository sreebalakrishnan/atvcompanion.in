Some reflections
Matsya Avatar as Ketu, the South Node of the Moon

Journey to Higher Knowledge
Matsya guides Satyavrata through a flood, to
safety, and to wisdom. Can we allow a Higher
Force to lead us in times of difficulty? How can
we do that?

Detachment and loss
Satyavrata had to let go of everything - his
kingdom, his people and what he considered to
be stable.. Can we stay detached? Maybe we
need to lose something to find something new?

Some reflections
Matsya Avatar as Ketu, the South Node of the Moon

Insight and knowledge
Matsya taught Vedic wisdom to the sages and to
Satyavrata. Is the universe trying to teach us
something, when we believe everything around
us is falling apart?

Huge transformation
Satyavrata becomes Vaivasvata Manu, a turning
point for the world. So the flood is an end to
something, and a beginning of something new..
A kind of a reset. How can we welcome a full
reset in our lives?

Some reflections
Matsya Avatar as Ketu, the South Node of the Moon

Disconnection from logic
The flood and storm and idea of a fish towing a
boat is a total disconnection from logic and
reality. Can we have faith in the illogical?

Independence
Satyavrata doesn’t cling to power, people or to
his kingdom. What are we clinging on to? How
can we release our grip on it?

Reflect on these themes to better understand
Ketu in your birth chart. More to come.
