SUN

Diameter: 1,391,000 km
Revolution: 225-250 million years
Rotation: 25 - 35 days (differential rotation)
Mass: 333,000 times the mass of Earth
Sanskrit: Surya / Ravi

Largest body in the solar system
Centre of the solar system
Everything revolves around the Sun
Source of all light and heat
Earth takes 365 days to go around
Orbits around a black hole

MOON

Diameter: 3,474 km
Revolution Period: 27.3 Earth days
Rotational Period: 27.3 Earth days
Mass: 0.0123x Earth
Sanskrit: Chandra

Tiniest body in the solar system
Reflects the light of the Sun
Appears as big as the Sun
Closest to Earth
Governs the tides
Illuminates the night
White in colour
Soft, pleasant and pleasing
Takes 28 days to go around the Earth
