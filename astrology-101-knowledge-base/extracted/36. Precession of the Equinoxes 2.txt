Ayanamsha

“How much the starting point of the Zodiac has shifted backwards”



Age of Pisces

The stars behind the intersection point is Pisces
Vernal Equinox is in Pisces
The Earth is wobbling, and the intersection point is shifting backward
When this shifts to Aquarius, it will be called the Age of Aquarius
It shifts around 50” a year
