Rama vs Krishna
Both served Dharma, but differently

Both are selfless, humble, equanimous and
balanced in the face of difficulties

Rama is the ideal human, Krishna is the wise
strategist

Rama prioritises Dharma, Krishna goes after
Adharma

Rama followed the rules even when it hurt him;
Krishna bent the rules to protect dharma

Rama had a conventional approach to decision
making, while Krishna strategized keeping the    3102 BC
end in mind
Rama vs Krishna
Rama renounced his kingdom, Krishna
embraces power, love, politics and war

Rama’s life is about sacrifice and renunciation;
Krishna’s life is about engaging with the world

Rama is about living rightly, Krishna is about
living wisely
