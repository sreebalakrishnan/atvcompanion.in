The legend


Once upon a time, there existed two magnificent civilizations, Lemuria and Atlantis, each known
for their wisdom, prosperity and technological advancements.

However, over time, the rulers grew corrupt and power-hungry.

The once-great civilizations were now under the oppressive rule of the Asuras.

As darkness spread, Brahma and Vishnu, the Gods of the Cosmos, were troubled. They knew that
they needed to save the Earth from complete ruin.

Desperate and not knowing what to do, they turned to Shiva Himself.

He was the Great Destroyer and the ultimate source of all Transformation.

He listened to Them, and agreed to end the virus that was spreading from Lemuria and Atlantis.

He prepared His divine bow and arrows.

He decided to end Lemuria first, and then Atlantis, knowing that this was necessary to end the
Asuras' reign of terror.

The legend (continued)


With the heavens trembling above, and the earth shivering below, Shiva released His mighty
arrows. One by one, they soared through the skies, guided by His divine vision.

The arrows struck Lemuria and Atlantis, piercing the very heart of these lands. As the arrows
found their mark, cataclysmic events were set into motion.

Earthquakes shook the foundations of Lemuria and Atlantis, and the oceans rose in fury. No
amount of prayer or repentance could change the course of events. The lands began to sink
beneath the waves, taking with them the asuras' rule and the corruption they had built.

As the civilizations sunk, balance was restored to the world.

The terrible rule of the asuras had ended, and the Gods once again watched over Earth with love
and care.

The story of Lemuria and Atlantis would be lost in time and shrouded in mystery, but it serves as a
reminder that even the mightiest can fall when evil takes their hold.

Lord Shiva restored harmony and balance, even though the process appeared destructive to all.

and Garuda's feathers.

His arrow takes 1.5 seconds to reach Lemuria.
