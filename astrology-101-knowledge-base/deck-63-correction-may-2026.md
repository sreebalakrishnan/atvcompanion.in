# Deck 63 — Correction Note (May 2026)

**Companion to:** `decks/63-interpreting-the-ascendant.md`
**Reason:** Deck 63 is older than `house-planet-interpretation-guidance.md`. Its 3-step Ascendant procedure compresses moves that the May 2026 guidance separates and, in places, **forbids**. This note flags the discrepancies so the cohort doesn't carry deck 63's habits into the newer procedure.

---

## What deck 63 teaches

Three steps for the Ascendant only:

1. Factual statement — Sign rises, Lord, Lord's placement sign, Lord's placement house.
2. Flashcard keywords — pull them for each factual element above.
3. Interpretive sentences — connect the keywords.

Worked example (Cancer Ascendant, Moon in Pisces 9H):

- Cancer = yin, cardinal, water → introvert, leader, emotions
- Moon = Krishna avatar
- Pisces = yin, mutable, water → introvert, adaptable, emotional
- 9th House = higher education

Sample sentences from the deck:
- *"I am emotional"*
- *"I am a leader"*
- *"My identity is linked to my higher education"*  ⚠
- *"I nurture and protect my feelings"*

---

## What the May 2026 guidance says about each sentence

| Sentence | What it combines | Verdict under guidance |
|---|---|---|
| *"I am emotional"* | Cancer (water → emotional) + 1H (self / "I am") | ✅ Legal — Step 1 of Path A: Sign + House. *(Sign building block + house keyword.)* |
| *"I am a leader"* | Cancer (cardinal → leader) + 1H (self) | ✅ Legal — Step 1 of Path A. |
| *"My identity is linked to my higher education"* | Moon (Lord of 1H → identity) + 9H (house-of-Lord's-placement → higher education) | ❌ **Forbidden.** Combines a Lord keyword (Step 4 territory) with a house-of-placement keyword (Step 2 territory) — two different sentence templates, never combined. |
| *"I nurture and protect my feelings"* | Moon (Krishna → nurture) + Pisces (water → feelings) | ✅ Legal — Step 4 of Path A: Ruler-of-House in Sign. *(Ruler keyword + sign building blocks; no house keyword.)* |

The forbidden sentence is the trap — it reads natural in English, lands meaningfully, and so it teaches the wrong combinatorial habit before the cohort has the new rules.

## How the deck 63 example reads when re-derived using the May 2026 8-step procedure (Path A)

**Subject:** the 1st house (the Ascendant). Cancer rises. Lord = Moon. Karaka = Sun. Moon sits in Pisces, in the 9th house.

### Step 1 — Sign + House
House keyword: *body / self*.

- Cancer (yin) + 1H (body) → *"My body holds itself in receptive ways."*
- Cancer (cardinal) + 1H (body) → *"My body initiates."*
- Cancer (water) + 1H (body) → *"My body moves with feeling."*

### Step 2 — Planet(s) in 1H
None placed in this example. Skip.

### Step 4 — Ruler of 1H in Sign
Lord = Moon, sits in Pisces.

- Moon (Krishna · nurture) + Pisces (yin) → *"I nurture from a quiet, inward place."*
- Moon (Krishna · nurture) + Pisces (mutable) → *"I nurture flexibly, adapting as the form shifts."*
- Moon (Krishna · nurture) + Pisces (water) → *"I nurture by feeling."*

### Step 5 — Karaka of 1H in Sign
Karaka of 1H = Sun. (In the Cancer-Ascendant example, the Sun's placement isn't given. Treat as TBD.)
*If the Sun were in (say) Sagittarius:* Sun (Rāma · vitality) + Sagittarius (yang / mutable / fire) → three sentences. **Never:** Sun (vitality) + 1H (body) — that's universal, true for all 8 billion.

### Forbidden sentence — re-derived legally
*"My identity is linked to my higher education"* breaks the rules. The legal way to surface the same insight:
- *"I nurture by feeling"* (Step 4 — Moon + Pisces water).
- *"In the 9H, I — through the Moon — am" — and stop there if you can't separate Step 2 from Step 4.* The 9H of placement is **not analysed** when the subject is the 1H. To bring the 9H in, change subject and run a fresh analysis on the 9H.

## Why this matters

The forbidden combination — *Lord-of-X-house keyword + house-where-the-Lord-sits keyword* — is the single most common confusion in Vikram's class (Session 41, April 19, 2026). Vikram repeatedly catches students collapsing two sentences into one. Deck 63 trains exactly the wrong reflex. The cohort needs to know:

- Deck 63 was a **first-pass exercise** before the rules tightened.
- Its sample sentence *"My identity is linked to my higher education"* is **not** how to write a 1H sentence under the May 2026 guidance.
- The clean replacement: keep Step 2 sentences (planet + house) separate from Step 4 sentences (ruler + sign) — and never use the Karaka with the house keyword.
