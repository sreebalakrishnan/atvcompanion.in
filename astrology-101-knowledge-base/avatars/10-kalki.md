## Astronomical / cosmological reading
From Deck #44 (Cosmology of Time): the current Kali Yuga began at midnight 17/18 February 3102 BC. Of its 432,000-year span, 5,126 years have elapsed and 426,874 years remain; Kali Yuga will end approximately in 428,899 AD. Six "Kings" / Shakhas are mapped onto its phases:
- Yudhistira Shakha: 3102 BC – 57 BC
- Vikrama Shakha: 57 BC – 78 AD
- Salivahana Shakha: 78 AD – 18,078 AD
- Vijayabhinandana Shakha: 18,078 AD – 20,078 AD
- Nagarjuna Shakha: 28,078 AD – 428,078 AD
- **Kalki Shakha: 428,078 AD – 428,899 AD**

Kalki is therefore positioned in the deck as the closing 821-year phase of Kali Yuga. (Source cited in deck: Sri Aurobindo, *Mother India*, April 2007.)
