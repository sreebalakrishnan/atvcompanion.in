Your suggestion · in action

Two modes

You suggested rolling content out in stages. So the page has two modes — a learner picks one.

◉ Learning
For first-time learners
Sections lock until the previous section's quiz is complete. The next section opens, the rest stay closed with a small "covered after Elements" poster. Pace is enforced.
○ Reference
For after the course
Everything available, all at once. No locks, no frontier. The page becomes a forever-companion for revisiting and looking things up.

One toggle in the masthead. Honors how a learner needs the page now vs how they'll need it after.
