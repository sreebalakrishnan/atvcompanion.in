Astrology 101

A companion app for Vikram's class. Holds the material we're learning, structured to come back to between sessions.

Built alongside Vikram's Astrology 101 cohort at allthingsvedic.in. Began as a stack of paper flashcards on a kitchen table — Sanchi and Sree's annual year-planning ritual — and grew into a single web page that holds the wheel of signs, the planetary cabinet, the avatar stories, the practices. Vikram has reviewed it; the cohort is now invited in. — Sree & Sanchi · alongside the class
