Read · beta

A companion for reading your own chart. Pick your Lagna, place your planets, and see the South Indian map of your life. The house-by-house walk-through follows.

§ One

Your Lagna — the rising sign

Tap the sign that was rising on the eastern horizon at your birth. (You'll find this on the chart Vikram cast for you, or any Vedic chart from Astrosage / Drik Panchang. It's also called Ascendant.)

§ Two

Your nine planets — which house each is in

For each Graha, pick the house number from your chart. If you don't know yet, leave it blank — you can come back and fill it in later. Vikram's nine: Sun, Moon, Mars, Mercury, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn, plus the lunar nodes Rahu & Ketu.
